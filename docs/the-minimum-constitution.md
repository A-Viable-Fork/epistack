# The Minimum Constitution

EpiStack is a minimal shared structure on which independent communities build knowledge that composes. It fixes only the invariants required for independently built epistemic systems to exchange work without importing one another's authority, and it leaves the rest, how to discover truth, validate sources, establish identity, weigh evidence, reach conclusions, with the communities and applications that hold the context. This document is why that boundary is where it is.

## Knowledge is what survives attenuation

Start with what knowledge is. A proof asks you to trust nothing about the prover: you check the steps, and a single checked proof removes its author entirely. A measured constant asks you to trust no single lab: what you trust is that independent measurements agree, so the number survives any one measurer being wrong. Both are the same operation run to different depths. Turn down your reliance on whoever is telling you, and knowledge is what does not move. A synthesis cannot perform this on itself, because removing its author destroys the result; it is a contribution to knowledge, one measurement's worth, and the layer that performs the turning-down is what has been missing.

The [full derivation is the climb](the-climb-of-synthesis.md); this document takes the definition as its ground and asks what infrastructure it requires.

## Trust in the producer was a well-calibrated basis

An accounting basis is built for a regime. It performs well while the conditions it was calibrated for hold, and it becomes inappropriate when they shift, not because it was mistaken but because the ground moved. Trusting the producer, believing a result because of who or what produced it and how carefully it presents, was such a basis, and for most of the history of recorded knowledge it was the right one. It worked because producing the marks of soundness cost roughly what soundness cost. Clean prose, cited sources, a confident and well-structured argument: these were expensive to fake and cheap to trust, so the reader's shortcut, a thing that looks rigorous probably is, tracked the truth well enough to carry the whole system. The channel from a producer to the record was never typed, and under those conditions it did not need to be. Trust-in-producer was operationally appropriate to its regime.

## The regime has shifted

Two facts move the ground. The first is old: humans are recursive modelers. We model the world, and we model other people modeling the world, which means any fixed signal a reader uses to decide trust becomes something a producer can model and aim at. A trust-heuristic is a reflex, a fixed if-it-looks-like-this-then-believe-it, and a reflex, once modeled, can be produced on purpose without the substance it used to certify. This was always true and always survivable, because producing the signal convincingly still took most of the work that the signal certified.

The second fact is what shifted: capable generation made the signal cheap while leaving the substance dear. The polish that meant someone had done the work is now producible without the work, at volume, each instance reading as cleanly as careful work once did. The regime condition that made trust-in-producer appropriate, signal-cost tracking substance-cost, no longer obtains. Nothing about the basis was wrong. Its conditions ended. And adding more trust-heuristics does not help, because each new heuristic is another fixed signal, another reflex a modeler can aim at; the harder the reader looks for marks of soundness, the more precisely those marks can be manufactured. Rebasing, not reinforcement, is what a regime shift calls for.

## What rebasing requires

The shift is a variety problem in the exact sense Ashby named: a regulator can govern a variable only while its variety meets the variety it must absorb. The variety of what arrives at the reader has overtaken the variety the reader's trust-heuristics can regulate, and there are only two moves, used together. Attenuate the variety the regulator must absorb, and amplify the regulator's own variety.

Producer-attenuation is the first move made structural. If a claim's standing recomputes from its cited support rather than from its producer, the reader no longer has to regulate the producer at all: the one variable that cheap generation floods, who-is-speaking-and-how-well, is removed from the computation. This is the unique move that cannot be gamed by a better-modeled signal, because it trusts no signal a modeler could forge; it trusts structure the reader recomputes. Typing the channel is the second move. A typed channel, where claims carry their kind and their support and their provenance, lets human judgment operate at scale on what actually bears weight, amplifying the regulator's reach instead of drowning it. Together these are what the definition of knowledge demanded all along: a layer that performs the attenuation, so that what survives it is what stands.

## The boundary, and why it falls where it does

Attenuation has two halves, and they belong to different parties. **Structural attenuation**, recomputing a claim's grade from its support without trusting its producer, is mechanical and the same everywhere, so it is fixed: a shared contract every participant holds, because a fixed mechanism here removes a target rather than creating one. It forges nothing; it only turns trust down. **Semantic attenuation**, judging whether the support is true of the world, is where the variety lives, and it is left free: each community's own work, expressed through the types it sets. Fixing it would be the jurisdiction error, installing one low-variety regulator where the world's variety requires many, and a single fixed judge of meaning is exactly the low-variety regulator that the high-variety world overwhelms or, being fixed, that a modeler learns to game. The boundary between fixed and free is the line between where mechanization removes a target and where it becomes one.

This submission enumerates both sides. The [contract register](contract-register.md) names what is fixed, each invariant with the check that enforces it; the [parameters register](parameters-register.md) names what is free, each parameter left to the community that holds the context. The boundary is not a matter of taste but an object you can read, and a linter holds the two apart.

## The question this submission asks

The submission's central question is therefore not whether EpiStack is a complete epistemic system. It plainly is not; completeness lives in the communities and applications built on it. The question is whether EpiStack identifies the right minimum constitution beneath complete epistemic systems: enough shared structure for their work to compound, and not so much shared judgment that pluralism collapses into one privileged synthesis. Fix too little and independent systems cannot exchange work without importing each other's authority; fix too much and the shared judgment becomes the single low-variety regulator the whole design exists to avoid. The claim is that the minimum is exactly the requirements of structural attenuation, and no more. Everything else in this repository is that claim, actualized and checkable.
