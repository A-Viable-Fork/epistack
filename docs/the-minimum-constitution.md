# The Minimum Constitution

EpiStack is a minimal shared structure on which independent communities build knowledge that composes. It fixes only the invariants required for independently built epistemic systems to exchange work without importing one another's authority, and it leaves the rest, how to discover truth, validate sources, establish identity, weigh evidence, reach conclusions, with the communities and applications that hold the context. This document is why that boundary is where it is.

## Knowledge is what survives attenuation

Start with what knowledge is. A proof asks you to trust nothing about the prover: you check the steps, and a single checked proof removes its author entirely. A measured constant asks you to trust no single lab: what you trust is that independent measurements agree, so the number survives any one measurer being wrong. Both are the same operation run to different depths. Turn down your reliance on whoever is telling you, and knowledge is what does not move. A synthesis cannot perform this on itself, because removing its author destroys the result; it is a contribution to knowledge, one measurement's worth, and the layer that performs the turning-down is what has been missing.

The [full derivation is the climb](the-climb-of-synthesis.md); this document takes the definition as its ground and asks what infrastructure it requires.

## Trust in the producer was a well-calibrated basis

An accounting basis is built for a regime. It performs well while the conditions it was calibrated for hold, and it becomes inappropriate when they shift. Trusting the producer, believing a result because of who or what produced it and how carefully it presents, was such a basis, and for most of the history of recorded knowledge it was the right one. It worked because producing the marks of soundness cost roughly what soundness cost. Clean prose, cited sources, a confident and well-structured argument: these were expensive to fake and cheap to trust, so the reader's shortcut, a thing that looks rigorous probably is, tracked the truth well enough to carry the whole system. The channel from a producer to the record was never typed, and under those conditions it did not need to be. Trust-in-producer was operationally appropriate to its regime.

## The regime has shifted

Two facts move the ground. The first is old: humans are recursive modelers. We model the world, and we model other people modeling the world, which means any fixed signal a reader uses to decide trust becomes something a producer can model and aim at. A trust-heuristic is a reflex, a fixed if-it-looks-like-this-then-believe-it, and a reflex, once modeled, can be produced on purpose without the substance it used to certify. This was always true and always survivable, because producing the signal convincingly still took most of the work that the signal certified.

The second fact is what shifted: capable generation made the signal cheap while leaving the substance dear. The polish that meant someone had done the work is now producible without the work, at volume, each instance reading as cleanly as careful work once did. The regime condition that made trust-in-producer appropriate, signal-cost tracking substance-cost, no longer obtains. Nothing about the basis was wrong. Its conditions ended. And adding more trust-heuristics does not help, because each new heuristic is another fixed signal to aim at; the harder the reader looks for marks of soundness, the more precisely those marks can be manufactured. Rebasing, not reinforcement, is what a regime shift calls for.

## The internet already ran this experiment

One seam first, because the parallel is disciplined by it. Packets carry no semantics and claims do, so the internet maps onto knowledge systems at the trust-architecture layer, how a system decides what to accept and from whom, and never at the content layer. Held at that layer, the history is exact. The early internet's protocols were trust-in-producer made mechanical: mail accepted the sender a message declared, routing accepted the path a neighbor announced, resolution accepted the first well-formed answer that arrived. And this was the correct engineering for its regime. The network was a small club of accountable institutions, producing trusted-looking traffic cost what producing legitimate traffic cost, and the one documented abuse of the era was answered by a reprimand delivered over the phone. The architects spent their scarce resources on surviving hardware failure rather than on verifying peers who were already accountable, and they were right.

Then the regime shifted, and it was the same shift. Commercialization dissolved the club, automation collapsed the cost of trusted-looking traffic, and the signal of soundness decoupled from soundness, on the network a decade before it happened to prose. The protocols did not change; the population arriving at them did. A worm propagated on cooperative trust files, one script reached five thousand forums in ninety minutes, a misconfigured router redirected the world's traffic for hours, and none of these was a failure of the design, only the design meeting inputs from outside the regime it was calibrated for.

The internet then made the other choice: it reinforced. Sender authentication, transport encryption, edge absorption, route attestation, each patch open, standardized, and effective, and each delegating a trust decision that had been distributed among peers to whichever operator could enforce it at scale. Deciding what mail is legitimate migrated to a few inbox providers, deciding which certificate binds a name migrated to an authority list a few browser vendors curate, deciding whether a site is reachable migrated to a few networks large enough to absorb attack. The degree is contested at the protocol layer, where standards remain open, and near undeniable at the enforcement layer, where the operational decisions sit. And the claim is computed rather than read in: each era, encoded faithfully as a kernel and run through this submission's conformance checker, holds the composition invariants, typed traffic and shared meaning by shared specification, and violates the control invariants, standing that recomputes rather than being granted. Where standing does not recompute, an authority must be installed to compensate, and an authority holding a trust decision is what centralization is. The two patches that restored recomputation instead of installing an authority are the two that look like this submission, and they are the slow ones, because rebasing after the shift pays the retrofit's full political price. (The dated record is grounded in the [lineage corpus](../corpora/lineage/); the derivation runs in the [counterexample exhibit](the-tcpip-counterexample.md).)

So the choice this document keeps naming has been made once already, at civilizational scale, the other way. Reinforcement secured the network and recentralized it; the patches worked and the platforms now own the trust they host. A knowledge system facing the same shift can reinforce, and inherit the same trajectory with claims in place of packets and the stakes raised by exactly the semantics packets never carried. Or it can rebase. What rebasing requires is next.

## What rebasing requires

The shift is a variety problem in the exact sense Ashby named: a regulator can govern a variable only while its variety meets the variety it must absorb. The variety of what arrives at the reader has overtaken the variety the reader's trust-heuristics can regulate, and there are only two moves, used together. Attenuate the variety the regulator must absorb, and amplify the regulator's own variety.

Producer-attenuation is the first move made structural. If a claim's standing recomputes from its cited support rather than from its producer, the reader no longer has to regulate the producer at all: the one variable that cheap generation floods, who-is-speaking-and-how-well, is removed from the computation. This is the unique move that cannot be gamed by a better-modeled signal, because it trusts no signal a modeler could forge; it trusts structure the reader recomputes. Typing the channel is the second move. A typed channel, where claims carry their kind and their support and their provenance, lets human judgment operate at scale on what actually bears weight, amplifying the regulator's reach instead of drowning it. Together these are what the definition of knowledge demanded all along: a layer that performs the attenuation, so that what survives it is what stands.

## The boundary, and why it falls where it does

Attenuation has two halves, and they belong to different parties. **Structural attenuation**, recomputing a claim's grade from its support without trusting its producer, is mechanical and the same everywhere, so it is fixed: a shared contract every participant holds, because a fixed mechanism here removes a target rather than creating one. It forges nothing; it only turns trust down. **Semantic attenuation**, judging whether the support is true of the world, is where the variety lives, and it is left free: each community's own work, expressed through the types it sets. Fixing it would be the jurisdiction error: a single fixed judge of meaning is a low-variety regulator set against the world's variety, so it is overwhelmed, and being fixed it is modeled, so it is gamed. The boundary between fixed and free is the line between where mechanization removes a target and where it becomes one.

**Identity attenuation** is the thread that crosses the line, which is why it needs naming rather than sorting. Producer attenuation removes who-is-speaking from a claim's standing, but the definition of knowledge readmits identity at exactly one point: independent measurements agreeing is an identity fact, a claim that two attestations came from distinct parties. Remove identity entirely and one actor with many hands prices as a crowd. So the fixed tier holds only the thin invariant: independence enters the computation as declared structure the gate prices, never as an inference the infrastructure performs, and same-party checking is admitted at its honest label instead of laundered into corroboration. Everything else about identity stays free, as the opening of this document left it: how a community establishes who someone is, at what level a contributor presents, what a persistent name is worth. Identity, like meaning, is judged in the free tier; what is fixed is that the judgment's output enters the grade as a declared claim rather than an assumed fact, so the reader can turn reliance on it down like everything else.

This submission enumerates both sides. The [contract register](contract-register.md) names what is fixed, each invariant with the check that enforces it; the [parameters register](parameters-register.md) names what is free, each parameter left to the community that holds the context. The boundary is not a matter of taste but an object you can read, and a linter holds the two apart.

## The question this submission asks

The submission's central question is therefore not whether EpiStack is a complete epistemic system. It plainly is not; completeness lives in the communities and applications built on it. The question is whether EpiStack identifies the right minimum constitution beneath complete epistemic systems: enough shared structure for their work to compound, and not so much shared judgment that pluralism collapses into one privileged synthesis. Fix too little and independent systems cannot exchange work without importing each other's authority; fix too much and the shared judgment becomes the single low-variety regulator the whole design exists to avoid. The claim is that the minimum is exactly the requirements of structural attenuation, and no more. Everything else in this repository is that claim, actualized and checkable.
