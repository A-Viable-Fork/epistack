# Submission Overview

### A Typed Substrate for Navigating Knowledge

A typed claim-graph and the loop that builds it. Every claim carries what a flat citation omits: its type, its verification state, and its provenance. The graph is built by one loop with two stations, an agent that multiplies candidates and a person who types them, closing at a gate where nothing promotes on its own authority. The organizing principle is requisite variety. Generation has outrun regulation, so the structure amplifies the regulator's variety and attenuates the variety it must absorb. The architecture is the familiar one of a store and an interface: a single canonical graph holds what is true, a typed API exposes it under a gate, and many clients build on that API, none able to write to truth except by passing the gate.

## Architecture

**The path.** A claim travels: proposed, then sound in a model, then checked against the world. The middle segment is a derivation, a traversal of transformations. The terminal is the last binding closure the claim has reached, and promotion is advancing along the path.

**Terminal types.** The closures a claim can reach, each naming who answers at that cut. On the path: a *measurement*, where the world closes a prediction the derivation produced (LHC safety runs here, every surviving branch closing against an astronomical observation); a *derivation*, where formal work legitimately stops with no measurement owed; a *withheld record*, a measurement behind a named sealed door, convertible when the record opens. Off the path: an *irreducible prior*, topping out in a reference-class choice no measurement reaches (the COVID dispute tops out here, priced and refused); a *constitutive* terminal, topping out in a framework adopted or refused (the welfare side of the eggs question). Meta: a *question set*, a claim about whether the graph's branches are all drawn (the eggs question, one prompt that is at least four).

**The typing rule.** A claim is typed by where it can terminate, and the type is the knife that carves a question into parts. Each part's terminal names who answers it: the world a measurement, a model run forward a derivation, a sealed dataset a withheld record, a commitment a constitutive question. The cuts the world or a model can answer route onward; the cuts a commitment answers are priced and refused.

**The loop.** The generation station multiplies candidates and sets the prior by declaring axioms and typing each claim. The gate promotes nothing on its own authority: agreement counts only once the agreeing parties are shown independent, and the rule names no agent, so it does not expire as the generators improve. The gate's composition across uncoordinated contributors is mechanical, demonstrated in `compose_gate.py`, which composes two emitters' frozen output with no model in the loop. The gate tests survival against attack, and a standing adversarial process supplies the attacks rather than waiting for them, so the map hardens as more scrutiny is spent on it.

**The corpora.** Three stores accumulate. The *atlas* is the derivation segment made queryable, structural expertise that lived in tacit form turned into external variety any agent can read. The *kill record* holds refuted claims, axiom-tagged. The *departure record* holds the places structural patterns fail real phenomena, the matched negative store to the kill record and the interface between the atlas and a measurement.

**Gaps.** What the map is missing is first-class, not an absence left to infer. A structural gap is detected mechanically: a claim whose support reaches no terminal, a dependency resting on a superseded source, a claim with no rebuttal searched, a question-set branch left undrawn. These carry the same standing as claims, objective and compounding, so the substrate tracks what it does not yet know and that tracking accretes too. The detector is an open API read, and run on the cases it reproduces by machine the structural gaps the project had been tracking by hand. Which gap most needs closing is a separate and subjective judgment, deferred to the assessment layer rather than asserted in the structure.

**The partition.** The two sides of the path. The auditable side, claims that cross a derivation to a measurement, is where a mechanical gate closes. The priced-and-refused side, the off-path terminals, is where no mechanical test reaches and judgment prices each side rather than audits it. The structure routes the first onward and refuses the second by construction, which is what grounds the no-verdict discipline.

**Composition.** The property the substrate exists to produce: uncoordinated contributions accrete onto one map instead of each run inventing a schema and discarding it. It is realized by one address space shared across contributors, the typed schema they all write to, and the gate that admits a claim only on independent corroboration. The site contract is emit and ingest, with no central merge; standing is two-leveled, a claim is open to anyone and judged on its grounding while changing the schema or the rules is staked against an earned record; a fork is a query not a copy, and disagreement is exposed and priced rather than reconciled. The rules govern structure and never content, so a more capable contributor can flood the claim layer, all of it judged the same way, and gain no power to rewrite the gate.

**Storage, a typed API, and clients.** The substrate is a store of knowledge with an interface over it, and it applies its own principle to that interface: canonical content, forkable everything else. Storage is the typed graph held as a tamper-evident, append-only history of patches, each a sealed canonical state, so a captured present cannot un-verify the past. Access is layered: a private personal layer where a claim is born, an open forum layer where anyone submits and engages but nothing carries verification weight, and the canonical layer, public to read and gated to write, the only claims that can ground derivation. The API is the engine: reads are open and read against a selected patch, resolve, decompose, compose, compare, query dependents, list gaps; a write is not a row inserted but a claim moved through a lifecycle until it seals into the next patch, and the credential that gate checks is standing, a non-transferable, decaying, time-locked record of verified labor that compute cannot front-load. Clients are the open forkable layer and they choose which patch to read against, so canonical is a client-side choice rather than a single owned store. Two depths: a thin client restyles the read response within a closed node-kind vocabulary; a fat client calls more of the API and composes its own way. Accessibility is a client concern, and a fork becomes a re-point rather than a rupture.

## Where it sits in the stack

The submission spans the three layers a full epistemic stack separates, with discipline about which component serves which.

**Ingestion.** Emit and the typing rule. A claim enters attributed to its source and typed at the moment it enters, and the gate's recognition of one claim arriving from several sources in different forms is an ingestion-side merge that becomes load-bearing later.

**Structure.** The path, the terminal types, and the corpora. The graph resolves which claims support which, carves one question into its sub-questions with the typing knife, and tracks its own evolution through the atlas, the kill record, and the departure record.

**Assessment.** The gate and the partition. The gate flags correlated evidence presented as independent and counts agreement only once independence is shown; the partition separates what a measurement settled from what a standoff only performed as settled, and prices the questions no evidence reaches.

**The interface, across all three.** The three layers store, structure, and judge; the engine exposes them as a typed API over a tamper-evident patch history, and clients consume it and touch none of it. Reads are open and read against a selected patch; a write is a claim earned through the gate, never a row inserted. How the map is used, and which patch is canonical, is held apart from whether what the map holds is true.

**Objective structure, subjective assessment.** The split the stack is built on. Structure and provenance, what supports what and who said it when, are objective and repeatable, so they compound across contributors who agree on nothing else; this is the store and its API. Assessing the best argument or the highest-priority gap turns on trust and chosen method, so it is subjective and deferred to late-binding clients that specialize and revise per a party's view, never written into the store. The same line decides where typing earns its place: the structure is typed because it must be mechanical for composition and gap-detection to work, while content and assessment keep their natural language. Rigid where structure must compound, free where judgment must stay open.

## Why capture is self-defeating

The system does not defend a single store; it makes one not worth owning. Two impossibility results bound any system of this shape, that an identity-free gate cannot prove source independence (Douceur) and that non-dictatorial aggregation is manipulable (Gibbard-Satterthwaite), and the design accepts them rather than claiming to beat them, pricing around them instead. The cost it imports is time: standing is duration of verified labor, the one axis synthesis cannot collapse, so to forge many independent corroborators an attacker must pay for many genuine time-locked contributions, at which price the attack is honest participation. Reputation is demoted to a fork-signal rather than an integrity guarantee, so it need not be unforgeable; a gamed reputation field is the readout that says fork now. Genuine cross-domain engagement leaves harmonic disagreement, so sterile agreement, not disagreement, is the alarm, and the clean consensus an attacker would manufacture is the suspicious one. The patch history is the backstop: a capture cannot un-verify the past, every prior patch's work stays canonical and forkable, so the cost of capture is bounded to the most recent window, and the honest minority re-points its clients at the last good patch. There is no single gate to own when canonical is a client-side choice over a tamper-evident history.

## Current state

The maturity of every claim here is graded in one place, `docs/status-ledger.md`. Built and verified is the typed substrate: the path, the typing rule and terminals, the partition, mechanical composition across two uncoordinated emitters, the corpora, the teaching and compare clients, the storage-API-clients boundary, forking, and structural gap detection, which reproduces by machine the structural gaps the sorry ledger tracked by hand. Designed and not yet built is the coordination layer the uncapturability argument rests on: the layered access, the time-locked standing economy, the Knowledge Game, the red-team immune system, and the patch history. Several seams in that design are open and named rather than hidden: whether time-locked labor can outprice synthetic labor under sampling, whether synthetic cross-domain disagreement is distinguishable from genuine, the integrity of the patch boundary as a rollback target, and a coordination-density lever surfaced in adversarial review. The substrate is demonstrated; the institution it is built for is designed, not yet standing.
