# Trust and View

A claim, once made, carries two things that are usually welded together and are in fact separable. One is its trust: the part of it that survives as trust in whoever asserted it is attenuated, the grounding that a second party can check without taking the author's word. The other is its view: the presentation of it, fitted to a particular reader, in a particular register, for a particular purpose. Trust is what makes a claim hold. View is what makes it legible to someone. They are different things, and the whole architecture rests on treating them as different things.

Synthesis fuses them. In a written synthesis, the party who establishes the trust is the party who presents it, and the two are inseparable in the artifact, because the grounding lives in the prose that carries it rather than in an object apart from the prose. This fusion is not a flaw in a particular synthesis; it is what synthesis is. And it has two consequences that are usually mistaken for separate limitations but are one. The grounding cannot be reused without the author, because it does not exist apart from the presentation that carries it, so the work does not compound: to build on a synthesis you re-read and re-reason it rather than standing on a checkable result. And no second view can rest on the same trust, because the trust is not a shared object, so there is no market of presentations over one grounding: each new view re-establishes the trust from scratch. A synthesis is a single-user artifact for one reason, that it fused the trust into the view.

The architecture's one move is to separate them. Establish the trust once, as a checkable object that a second party can verify without trusting its author, and let the view be anything built over that object. That single cut is the whole of it, and everything the architecture claims is a consequence of the cut taken to one side or the other.

Taken to the trust side, the cut yields compounding. If the grounding is a checkable object rather than prose, it is reused without its author, so work accretes: a result established once is stood on rather than redone, and a correction propagates rather than being re-litigated in every document that relied on it. Compounding is not an added feature; it is what the trust side of the cut does.

Taken to the view side, the cut yields a market. If the trust is shared and settled, then many views can be built over it, by many parties, none of whom has to be trusted about the grounding, because the grounding is not theirs to touch. A reader moves among those views freely and never pays the trust cost twice. This market competes on how to present the knowledge while agreeing on the knowledge, which is why it does not fragment truth the way markets of information usually do: there is nothing left for it to fragment, because the trust was separated out and made common. A market of views is not an added feature either; it is what the view side of the cut does.

Taken to the level of what makes a claim hold at all, the cut completes into a definition of knowledge itself. Knowledge is the invariant left as the model is attenuated. The way a claim is made to hold is by replacing trust in the knower with a warrant that does not need the knower, and typing is how the replacement is performed: to type a claim is to subject it to that type's attenuation, driving the claim's dependence on its author down toward a floor. The type sets the form the attenuation takes, and there are two forms, split by whether the type can secure itself.

Where a type self-secures, the attenuation completes to zero, and a mathematical proof is the pure case. A valid proof asks you to trust nothing about the prover, only to check the steps, so a single proof plus its review subtracts the prover entirely, because what establishes the theorem is the verification and not the prover's authority, and the prover could be anyone or no one. Subtraction is attenuation's complete form, reached only by self-securing types, and it is exactly what the grounding rule does to a claim that can be checked: the gate verifies rather than trusts, which is peer review made mechanical.

Where a type does not self-secure, the attenuation approaches a floor without reaching zero, and a measurement is the case. One physicist measuring a quantity is a declaration you either trust or do not, and no single measurement removes the measurer; convergence does the attenuating instead, driving dependence on any one measurer down across many independent measurements toward a floor that still rests on the measurements, so the knowledge is what survives any one of them being wrong. The safety of a collider is not any one team's finding; it is what remains standing when every team's authority is attenuated and only the structure of their agreement is left. One measurement is testimony; many, composed so that no measurer is load-bearing, are knowledge.

This places synthesis, and the placement is generous rather than dismissive. Knowledge is a hold, a synthesis a reach: knowledge is composed of syntheses and identical to none, and it is the established ground each new synthesis starts from, so the two are a cycle, every hold once a reach and every reach starting from holds, no reach itself a hold because a hold bears weight independent of the line that found it. A synthesis is one reasoner's act of reading the record and reaching a view, the individual epistemic act that any body of knowledge is composed from, indispensable because someone has to make the claims, and it stands to knowledge as one measurement stands to the measured constant. So the critique of synthesis is not that it is weak but that it is one viewpoint, and one viewpoint is not knowledge until the synthesizer is attenuated out, which is the move a lone synthesis cannot make on itself. A synthesis fuses production and presentation into one artifact, and the kernel splits that artifact along the same cut it draws everywhere: the produced claims compose into knowledge on the trust side, their author attenuated out, and the presentation becomes one view among many on the view side. The kernel does not compete with synthesis. It is the layer that lets many syntheses compound into knowledge instead of ending as single-user artifacts.

Verification and convergence are two forms of the one typing-attenuation, differing because the types differ in whether they self-secure. So the composition is not statistical averaging of noisy reports, which is only the weaker fallback used where checking is impossible; it is the replacement of trust with a knower-independent warrant, and a verified proof is the cleanest instance of the whole thesis, a claim whose attenuation has completed and whose author is gone. This is what science already does, slowly and socially, through review and replication, and knowledge in the scientific record is the part that survives any individual scientist being wrong. The architecture's contribution is to make both forms properties of a structure rather than outcomes of social time: the gate verifies what self-secures, composition attenuates the knower out of the rest, and a synthesis can perform neither form, because its synthesizer is never verified and never subtracted, only trusted, and subtracting it destroys the result.

The bound is precise, because the claim is strong only if it is exact. This produces knowledge in the specific sense of knower-independent warranted standing, not truth. The structure does not manufacture true claims; it mechanizes the verification and the convergence that a scientific community already treats as where knowledge lives, so that what review and replication achieve over years becomes a property the structure carries. Knower-independence is the output, not truth.

The cut also separates the claim from the nature of whoever made it, and this is the same separation seen once more. If trust is a property of a claim's structure, checkable by a second party, then it does not depend on whether the claim came from a person, an organization, a model, or a pipeline of these, because the checkable core examines the claim and not the agent. So the boundary the architecture draws is not human on one side and machine on the other; it is checkable-core on one side and fallible-producer on the other, where a producer is anything that emits claims. Any policy about which producers may do which things is a choice a community configures, not a fact the architecture imposes, precisely because the architecture already checks every producer's claims the same way. Producer-agnosticism is not a further commitment; it is the cut applied to the question of who asserts.

Everything downstream is this one cut expressed for a particular job. The critique of synthesis is the observation that it fuses trust and view. The kernel is the object that holds the trust apart from any view. The periphery is where the views live. Composition is the cut extended across trust bases. The assessment capabilities are the computations a checkable trust object makes possible. And the account of scaling, of compounding, of a diverse ecosystem of tools, is the market and the compounding that the two sides of the cut yield. Separate trust from view, and the rest follows.
