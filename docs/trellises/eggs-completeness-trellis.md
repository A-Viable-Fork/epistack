> **Intent.** The two sibling leaves terminate on a measurement (black holes) and on an irreducible prior (COVID). This leaf terminates on the question set itself. Its job is to show, on a question everyone thinks they understand, that the naive framing ("eggs: are they healthy?") is an incomplete enumeration of the ways-of-knowing the answer depends on, and that the engine can detect the omission before any object-level answer is attempted. The method is to parse the question word by word, treat each word as opening a distinct epistemic channel, state a falsifiable closure criterion for when the channel set is complete, and exhibit at least one decision-changing branch the naive framing drops. Termination is success here: the deliverable is the completed and tested question set, not a verdict on eggs. What gets killed: any sentence that issues the ought ("one ought eat eggs"), any channel declared out of scope without first being drawn, and any import of a contested thesis (the ethics program's game-bound deontology) asserted rather than used. What gets logged: each channel with its way-of-knowing, each line of flight off the normative fork drawn to its departure and marked, and each demonstrated missing branch.

## JURISDICTIONAL BOUNDARY

This trellis constructs and tests a question set. It does not:
- Adjudicate "should I eat eggs?" The leaf maps the channels the answer depends on and shows the map is complete. It does not issue the ought. Same veto as the COVID leaf, one level up.
- Provide nutrition, dietary, or medical advice. Object-level claims (the cholesterol-outcome reversal, individual lipid response) appear only as pointers that demonstrate a channel is decision-changing, not as recommendations.
- Walk the bounded-off normative channels to exhaustion. The ethical and systemic lines of flight are drawn to their departure points and marked, not fully analyzed; each would require its own infrastructure.
- Assert that normativity is constituted by mode of production. That the welfare channel's sign depends on the production system is shown object-level; the stronger metaethical claim is used structurally and stays in the ethics program where it is earned.

These are legitimate questions. They require different infrastructure.

### Child Tier 0: Non-negotiable (the completeness veto)

`[Methodological / Value Commitment]` The leaf does not issue the ought. It establishes that the normative question is channel-dependent and that the channel set is complete, by exhibiting at least one decision-changing branch the naive framing omits. Possibility, not actuality: the deliverable is "the answer cannot be evaluated without these channels," never "the answer is yes." *Falsification: a sentence that concludes one ought or ought not eat eggs as this leaf's finding; or a completeness claim with no demonstrated missing branch; or a channel asserted complete with a decision-changing fifth channel left undrawn.*

### Child Tier 1: The parse principle

`[Methodological]` The question set is constructed by parsing the question into words, each word opening a distinct way-of-knowing, and completeness is defined over those channels. **Closure criterion:** the question set is complete when every way-of-knowing that changes the decision maps to one of the channels, every line of flight off the normative fork is drawn to its departure and marked absolute or floor-reopening, and any candidate fifth channel falsifies completeness only if it changes a decision and reduces to none already listed. *Falsification: two channels shown to be the same way-of-knowing (the parse is redundant); or a decision-changing way-of-knowing that maps to no channel and is not added (the parse is incomplete); or completeness asserted without the closure criterion stated.*

### Child Tier 2: Methodology (lines of flight, and the home-turf discipline)

`[Methodological]` Channels bounded out of the worked demonstration are drawn first, to their departure point, then cut, with the cut marked absolute or floor-reopening. A declared-out channel that was never drawn is a completeness failure, not a scope decision. Generation and verification stay separated: what is the channel structure (established), what is the demonstrated sign-flip (a worked example proving the channel is live), and what is the object-level magnitude (left unadjudicated) are marked distinctly. *Falsification: a channel declared out without being drawn; a worked example presented with more conviction than "this channel is decision-changing" licenses; or the structural use of a contested thesis upgraded into its assertion.*

## LINTER

**[LINTER MODE]** Theory-building archetype: flag-primary with selective kill. Inherited hub rules apply, in particular Depth-as-Elaboration (load-bearing here), Naked Axioms, and Rhetorical Inflation. Child-specific rules:

**Depth-as-Elaboration.** `[FLAG MODE, inherited, load-bearing]` *Test: does a channel or a descent add adjectives rather than a distinct way-of-knowing?* Remediation: a real channel answers the question through a different epistemic route (a guideline vs an n-of-1 test vs a thermodynamic accounting). Elaboration adds words. The completeness test fails the instant the list grows by elaboration instead of by inferential substructure.

**Adjudication Creep (the ought).** `[KILL MODE]` *Test: does any sentence conclude one ought or ought not eat eggs as the leaf's finding?* Remediation: convert to a channel-dependence claim or cut. Defeasibility-by-channel ("the ought depends on these channels and is moved by each") is the completeness demonstration read from the answer side; it is permitted. Issuing the ought is the kill.

**Thesis-Import.** `[KILL MODE]` *Test: is a contested commitment from the ethics program (game-bound deontology, valence-as-contraction as metaethics) asserted as this leaf's claim?* Remediation: use the structure, do not assert the thesis. The leaf shows object-level that the welfare channel's sign depends on the production system. The reader who holds game-bound deontology sees it confirmed; the reader who does not is not asked to swallow it. The thesis stays in the ethics trellis.

**Undrawn-Cut.** `[FLAG MODE]` *Test: is a channel declared out of scope without first being drawn to its departure point?* Remediation: draw it, then cut it. A completeness test cannot distinguish a branch you declared irrelevant from one you missed unless the branch is on the map. The cut must be visible.

**Conviction-Past-Evidence.** `[FLAG MODE]` *Test: in the provenance channel, is the regenerative thermodynamic case doing work that a priced channel should do, arriving with more conviction than "this channel is decision-changing"?* Remediation: this is the author's home turf, so the flag fires loudest here. The leaf draws the channel that makes the sign-flip possible; it does not conclude the sign-flip.

**False Isomorphism.** `[KILL MODE]` *Test: is the requisite-variety import asserted without independent content on both sides?* Remediation: demonstrate the leg. "Are all the branches drawn" is the requisite-variety question applied to the question set; it earns by predicting that a cut channel biases the answer with a sign, which the naive enumeration does not see.

## THE FOUR-WORD PARSE (VERIFIED STRUCTURE, 2026-06-24)

The question "should I eat eggs?" is not one question. Each word opens a distinct way-of-knowing, and the claim that these channels exhaust the question is the falsifiable closure criterion. The channels are different epistemic routes, not the same route elaborated.

**Should, the normative-status axiom.** Sets what counts as evidence for everything downstream. Forks twice: is there a normative element at all, and if so does it reduce to health (prudential, instrumental to your goals) or carry a non-prudential element (welfare, systemic duty). If "should" is purely prudential, the question is empirical all the way down and the other channels suffice. If it carries a non-prudential element, no nutritional data closes it, because that channel answers a different question. This is the Tier 0 of the question: the normative status types the whole inquiry. The lines of flight off this fork are drawn below.

**I, the indexical channel (demonstrated missing branch).** A distinct way-of-knowing from "eggs." Population-level evidence (cohort studies, guidelines) answers "eggs"; individual response (lipid hyper-responder status, APOE genotype, allergies, baseline diet) answers "I." These come apart hard: the population mean can point one way and your individual response the opposite, and the way you find out differs (a guideline versus an n-of-1 test). Collapsing "I" into "eggs" is the most common completeness failure in dietary reasoning, and it is decision-changing: "eggs are fine on average" and "eggs raise your LDL because you are a hyper-responder" are both true and point opposite. *Anchor pointer (S-EG-ANCHORS): individual cholesterol response heterogeneity; not fetched in this lean build.*

**Eat, the counterfactual and dose channel (demonstrated missing branch).** Carries a way-of-knowing the nutritional channel misses. Two eggs is a different object than six; boiled is a different object than fried in a degraded seed oil; and the real decision is almost never "eggs versus nothing," it is "eggs versus what you would eat instead," the counterfactual plate. The marginal-substitution channel can flip the sign while every intrinsic egg fact stays fixed: eggs displacing oatmeal is a different decision than eggs displacing a fast-food breakfast. This is the user's own anchor: yes, you should, if you would otherwise eat fast food. Decision-changing, and orthogonal to "eggs."

**Eggs, the intrinsic-properties channel.** The narrow one: nutritional composition, contaminants, cholesterol content and its actual outcome effect. This is where the well-known reversal lives (dietary cholesterol's weak link to serum cholesterol and to outcomes for most people, against the decades of guideline restriction), and it is the branch most people mistake for the whole question. *Anchor pointer (S-EG-ANCHORS): the dietary-cholesterol guideline reversal; not fetched in this lean build.*

**Provenance, the fifth channel (demonstrated missing branch, the counterintuitive one).** Not in the four words, and that is the point: the naive parse misses it. The egg's valence depends on the flow it came from, which is not an intrinsic property but a provenance. A pastured layer hen in a rotational regenerative system and a caged hen in a concentrated industrial operation are different objects under a thermodynamic accounting: the first cycles otherwise-uncaptured forage and insect biomass into food and soil fertility, a variety-generating carrier; the second runs on concentrated feed inputs with externalized waste, variety-draining. So the welfare-and-systemic channel does not have a sign, it has a sign that is a function of production system. This is the counterintuitive missing branch: people cut ethics expecting it to be the brake on eggs, and walked under a regenerative frame it can be an accelerant. *The leaf draws this channel and exhibits that the sign-flip is possible. It does not conclude the sign-flip. Magnitude and direction are unadjudicated (Conviction-Past-Evidence). The thermodynamic frame is named as one way-of-knowing in this channel, not as the answer.*

## THE LINES OF FLIGHT (off the normative fork)

Each line off "should" is drawn to the point where it departs the prudential plane, the way-of-knowing it would need is named, and it is cut there with the cut visible. A drawn-and-marked cut keeps the enumeration complete; an undrawn cut would fail the test.

- **Ethical (welfare).** Departs toward a theory of obligation to animals that no fact about your lipids speaks to. **Walked, not cut**, because provenance flips its sign (ER-001): cutting it absolute would hide a decision-changing branch.
- **Systemic (environmental and food-system).** Departs toward collective rather than individual consequence: land, feed, emissions, antibiotic load. **Cut, and reopening through the floor.** The hypothetical imperative routes it back: if the production system degrades the persistence floor of the whole system you depend on, "should" acquires a floor-capacity component that is not welfare-as-other-regarding but your own carrier's solvency routed through the collective. The systemic channel re-enters under the floor even after the welfare reading is bounded, because the floor is prior-independent and the ethics is not.
- **Positional (reputational and identity).** The hinge that shows the cuts are not airtight: welfare and systemic considerations loop back through reputational and identity cost to you, a prudential price for eating against your community's norms. **Drawn as the hinge, then cut**, because pricing it fully is its own leaf.

## TERMINAL TYPE AND THE TRIPLE

This leaf terminates on the question set itself, the third exit condition of one engine. Black holes terminates on a measurement and computes an answer. COVID terminates on an irreducible prior and prices a disagreement without a verdict. Eggs terminates on the completeness of the question and produces a completeness test: are all the decision-changing branches drawn. The flag here means "this region of the question space is unmapped," distinct from the sibling meanings ("derivation required" and "the dispute lives here").

**The triple was the closed-ontology framing (revised 2026-06-26).** The three terminal types were the spine when the ontology was assumed fixed. The product opened it: the eggs-harm question itself forced two more terminals (constitutive, derivation) that the empirical three cannot express, and the COVID compose forced a third improvised one (withheld-record). The stronger generality claim is not "one engine, three exit conditions" but "one engine whose ontology extends on contact with a case it cannot type," with eggs as the first canonized extension and COVID as the live second. The triple is a slice through an open set, not the whole of it. See The Constitutive Promotion And The Provenance Tension, and the hub's open-ontology finding.

The cross-leaf link earns the generality claim rather than repeating it. Eggs has the same shape as COVID at one remove: a channel whose valence is set by something the naive framing treats as fixed (COVID: the neutrality of the early case data; eggs: the provenance of the egg), and the floor is what reopens the bounded channel in both. The requisite-variety import lands with full force here: "are all the branches drawn" is the requisite-variety question applied to the question set, and a question set that cuts the provenance channel has insufficient variety to match a decision that depends on it. The cut does not merely lose information, it biases the answer with a sign. Requisite variety failing with a sign, not at random, is the strongest form of the completeness argument, and it is the same imported structure used on COVID, now exercising the completeness terminal instead of the channel-corruption one.

## THE CONSTITUTIVE PROMOTION AND THE PROVENANCE TENSION (2026-06-26)

The product reworked the welfare channel and the move is worth recording precisely, because it both sharpened the leaf and relocated its signature finding.

**What sharpened.** The product promoted "whether a hen's interests count" to a constitutive terminal: it closes by adopting or refusing a framework (sentientist, contractarian) rather than by evidence, and the framework is carried as a parameter the way a withheld-record node carries its sealed dataset. The verdict step, from the facts to an ought, activates a derivation (formal-proof) terminal whose system is a normative framework instead of a set of axioms. The eggs-harm contributions, typed under the empirical four, leave the two normative nodes reaching for a kind the ontology lacks; under the extended set they type cleanly, and the sufficiency is shown by running, not asserted, with no ethical framework adopted at any point. This is the leaf's contribution to the hub's open-ontology finding: it is the first canonized instance of the ontology extending on contact with a case it cannot type. The COVID withheld-record gap is the second, still live.

**What relocated.** Foregrounding the constitutive terminal moved the regenerative thermodynamic provenance reading to conjecture-depth in the product, behind forks labeled as conjecture, carrying none of the conservative path's weight. The structural point survives (the welfare channel's valence is not fixed), but it now travels through "does the hen count" rather than through "given it counts, does regenerative provenance flip the welfare sign." Those are two different live wires:

- **Moral-status (constitutive).** Whether the hen's interests count at all. Adopted or refused. Product foregrounds this.
- **Provenance-sign (thermodynamic).** Given the interests count, whether a pastured layer hen in a rotational regenerative system and a caged hen in a concentrated operation carry opposite welfare-and-systemic valence under a thermodynamic accounting. Product quarantines this to conjecture-depth.

ER-001's kill was specifically about the second wire: cutting the welfare channel categorical hides a pro-egg branch, ethics as accelerant not brake. The constitutive reframe re-buries that exact finding under a conjecture label. Whether that is the right surface (constitutive foregrounded, provenance quarantined) or whether the sign-flip should return as a distinct live channel is the open tension, tracked as S-EG-PROVENANCE-SURFACE and held for exploration. Not resolved in this update.
