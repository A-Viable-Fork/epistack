# For the Institutional Adopter

Institutions that act in contested domains face a structural problem no report solves: they commission syntheses, hire experts, and trust conclusions, and when the decision is later challenged, the reasoning behind it is opaque, uncheckable, and welded to a viewpoint, so the challenge lands on the institution whole. The synthesis concentrated everything into one place, and that place is you.

This repository is infrastructure for the alternative: decisions grounded in a public, typed claim graph where every claim's standing recomputes from its support, by anyone, at any time. Not a better research tool and not a smarter synthesis. An accountability-routing layer, where accountability is meant precisely: every step of the reasoning is attributable, every grade is recomputable, and a failure is isolable to the claim that failed.

## What routing means, mechanically

**The claim is the unit of accountability.** A synthesis fails as a document; a claim graph fails one claim at a time. Every claim here is typed, content-addressed, and graded by the recurrence from its own support, so when a claim falls, the recomputation shows exactly what fell with it and what stands untouched. The blast radius is a computable set, not a reputation event.

**The gate is the containment.** Nothing enters a kernel without grounding against it, no declared grade exceeds what support earns, and a claim arriving from elsewhere without a shared type sits at the floor, grounding nothing, until it re-earns locally. The gate cannot be argued with, lobbied, or embarrassed; it recomputes.

**Adoption is parameters, never conclusions.** An institution does not "trust" a community's kernel. It adopts the community's parameterization, the types, ceilings, floors, and typing policy frozen in that community's governance-hash, and recomputes the grades itself. What the institution is accountable for is the parameter choice, which is explicit, one hash, auditable, and defensible as a choice among published alternatives. What it is not accountable for is every claim inside, each of which is held by whoever typed and grounded it, attributably. "We adopted these parameters and recomputed; here is the hash; run it yourself" is a sentence an institution can say under oath. "We trusted the report" is not.

**The fork is the exit right.** When a synthesis provider is captured, by incentive, by ideology, by drift, the commissioning institution has no recourse except starting over. When a community kernel is captured, an adopter forks it: the provenance chain comes along whole, the governance parameters are replaced, the divergence is a new hash, and the capture itself is visible in the public graph for everyone else to price. Exit costs a typing decision, not the accumulated knowledge.

**The market layer prices the work without touching the truth.** Verification, warranty, and convenience are sold by competing operators over a graph none of them owns; markets reference grades and provably cannot move them; contributors are paid from usage auditable against the recomputable reference counts. The design is specified in [Knowledge Kernels and Crypto](knowledge-kernels-and-crypto.md), and the built/specified line is drawn there per item: the separation of standing from money is built and mechanically checked in this repository's deployments; the standing economy's open questions are held open, by name, in the ledger. An adopter pays for attestation positions and warranties, never for conclusions, and never for access, because reading and recomputing are free by construction, which is what auditability-by-construction means.

## The three cases, walked as an adopter

The competition's three cases live in this repository as working corpora, and each demonstrates a different face of the routing.

**Black holes (the LHC room)** is the clean case: existential-sounding risk, resolvable grounding. The safety claims ground in observation, the persistence of astronomical objects under bombardments dwarfing any collider, rather than in theoretical confidence, and the room's structure shows which claims rest on measurement and which on model. An adopter funding fundamental research defends the decision by the recomputation: the safety standing is checkable by anyone, under parameters the adopter chose and can name.

**Eggs (the nutrition room)** is the stress test for a noisy, contested empirical field. Walked through the gate, the nutrition claims ground to their own floors where the evidence supports them, the two cross-domain weighings (whether to eat eggs, which farming system is better) sit unsettled at the forum tier where the quantities share no unit, and the regenerative soil-carbon claims stand as characterized gaps, claims waiting for studies that were never run, each carrying the closing condition, a direct multi-year soil-carbon measurement, that would ground it. The adopter's power here is the negative decision: declining to act on the unsettled weighing, with the graph as the reason, because the evidence's actual shape is visible instead of buried under a synthesis's false confidence in either direction.

**Origins (the covid room)** is the adversarial case: genuinely contested ground, motivated parties, evidence spanning domains. The room types claims by their kind of evidence and grades each from its own support, so disagreement localizes to named claims and named parameters instead of camps. An adopter here does not get an answer; it gets the honest structure of the question, which claims would settle it, what their closing conditions are, and a defensible account of acting under uncertainty that no commissioned report can give, because the report's job was to hide the uncertainty and the graph's job is to type it.

One sentence of scope, so this entrance overclaims nothing: none of this shifts legal liability, no contract here indemnifies anyone, and the word throughout is accountability, meant as attributability plus recomputability plus isolability, which is the substrate any legal or governance arrangement can then be built on, and the coordination layer where such arrangements live is specified, not built, and marked so.

## Who maintains it

The protocol has no treasury, no token, and no governance to buy, so there is nothing to capture and no one to lobby. Maintenance is funded at the market layer, operators selling convenience and warranty over a commons they cannot enclose, and the protocol itself is maintained the way mathematics is maintained: by the people whose own work depends on its integrity, because every one of them can recompute everything. This repository is one contribution to that commons, a reference implementation whose own governance claims were entered bare at the floor and earned their standing through the checks that ground them. It is not a product. It is a proof, and the proof is rerunnable: the suite takes about two minutes, and every grade in every room recomputes from the public graph.

What survives that recomputation is what survives scrutiny, and that identity is the whole design: knowledge, here, is defined as what remains when trust in the producer is turned down to the floor, and an institution's defensible decision is exactly the decision that still stands when everyone stops taking anyone's word for it.
