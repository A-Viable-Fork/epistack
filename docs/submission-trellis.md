> **Operational Intent.** This trellis governs production of a single FLF submission. The method nests as a work-hierarchy: the meta-trellis as generator and spec, this submission as the same method instantiated, and worked case fragments (black holes, COVID, eggs) as the executed leaves. The work-hierarchy is the function. The prose stays flat: a stranger reads a single legible spec that points into the leaves, not three layers of meta-structure. The objective is an entry that clears the top prize tier and opens the continuation relationship; the floor is that no element may carry more persuasive weight than evidential weight. The win condition is the external compounding protocol (S-INTEROP), the one thing FLF prizes most. Elements that violate the normative floor are killed. Open uncertainties and the interoperability gate are logged.

## JURISDICTIONAL BOUNDARY

This trellis addresses production of one competition entry. It does not address:
- Building a production-grade, fully-featured epistemic system. FLF explicitly asks for advances on components, not a complete system.
- Adjudicating COVID-19 origins as a first-order scientific question. The verdict is an output of the demonstrated method, not a claim this trellis asserts.
- Securing the continuation-funding relationship. That sits downstream at roughly 75 percent even for top entries, outside this trellis's control.
- Resolving the metaphysics of truth. This trellis commits operationally to value-and-verification pragmatism and stops there.

These are legitimate questions. They require different infrastructure.

## KNOWN CONSTRAINTS (FROM THE BRIEF AND RULES)

Confirmed from the competition page and the rules instrument. Unconfirmed items are tracked as sorries.

- **Format.** The written discussion targets ten pages, excluding appendix material, worked examples, and tools. Worked examples and example knowledge bases may be arbitrarily sized within reason but must be navigable, with curated pointers to the effective regions. Code is either brief legible pseudocode or one-click runnable. Interactive artifacts are admissible and close to the shape FLF asks for, which clears the progressive-disclosure tool (S-SCROLLER) on format.
- **Two-case floor.** The spec route requires demonstration on parts of at least two cases. Three are scoped; at least two must be executed for real.
- **Continuation is opt-in.** Funding beyond the prize is negotiated case by case, raised with finalists after judging, and is an incubation relationship. Entering commits to nothing downstream by itself.
- **License clause unread.** The binding IP terms sit in a gated rules doc. Reading the license-grant clause before submitting is tracked as S-IP-CHECK, because the asset is a general methodology that may be published or extended across other programs.

### Child Trellis Constraints (The Submission Layer)

**Tier 0 (Child): Hard vetoes.** `[Value Commitment]` An element is killed instantly if its persuasive weight exceeds its evidential weight, if it asserts the proxy-target wedge is zero, or if it adjudicates a mind-independent case via utility rather than evidence. *Falsification mode: a reader can point to a sentence that would move a judge more than the evidence behind it warrants, or to a claim that the prize and the truth are the same target, or to a verdict on COVID origins grounded in usefulness rather than evidence. Any one is a kill.*

**Tier 1 (Child): The mapping principle.** `[Methodological Commitment]` A trellis is an epistemic stack, and the correspondence is stated explicitly rather than asserted: tier structure maps to the assessment layer, the Compost Pile maps to provenance of ruled-out claims, the Discharge Ledger maps to calibrated uncertainty and cruxes, the Linter maps to the ingestion and quality gate, the Conversational Dyad maps to interoperability and compounding. *Falsification mode: any leg of the mapping that cannot be demonstrated on a worked fragment is struck from the submission rather than claimed.*

**Tier 2 (Child): Methodology.** `[Methodological Commitment]` Parts of at least two cases are executed for real, not sketched. Generation and verification are separated within the writeup itself. Cruxes are disclosed for audit; tactics are withheld. The pragmatist axiom must cash in the worked example, which means usability shown serving re-verification plus at least one logged instance of declining a rhetorically strong, evidentially weak move. *Falsification mode: a demonstration that names a failure mode without discharging it, or a writeup that collapses generation and verification into one confident narrative, or a pragmatist claim that never pays out in a worked fragment.*

## LINTER

**[LINTER MODE]** Operational archetype: flag-primary with selective kill. Most failures produce a flag and a revision instruction. Hard violations of the normative floor produce a kill.

**Meta-Flex Trap.** `[KILL MODE]` *Test: is self-reference presented for credit, the hub foregrounded as the deliverable, or cleverness about winning made the headline?* Remediation: relocate the derivation to the design-rationale layer, restore the meta-trellis as the true root, lead with the executed leaf.

**Promissory Note.** `[KILL MODE]` *Test: does an element terminate in "and therefore I should go do the work" where the work was the requirement?* Remediation: do the work, or cut the element. A plan where execution was demanded is a kill.

**Proxy-Truth Conflation.** `[KILL MODE]` *Test: does any claim state that the prize and the truth are one target, that winning simply requires truth?* Remediation: replace with directional alignment plus pragmatist accounting of the residual wedge.

**Rhetorical Inflation.** `[KILL MODE]` *Test: does a sentence persuade past its evidence?* This is FLF's own stated screen and the pragmatist red line at once. Remediation: cut the inflation or attach the missing evidence. A note that wins now and fails to cash later is truth-shaped until the verification comes due.

**Census-Without-Discharge.** `[FLAG MODE]` *Test: does the enumeration of failure modes run longer or hotter than the proof that they were cleared?* Remediation: attach a discharge to each named failure mode, weight the artifact so the doing is unmistakably the larger half. A failure mode plus its discharge reads as mastery; a failure mode alone reads as a warning written about oneself.

**Tactics Disclosure.** `[FLAG MODE]` *Test: does the prose narrate rubric-engineering rather than disclose a load-bearing uncertainty?* Remediation: convert to crux disclosure or cut. Disclose the doubt, not the stagecraft.

**Metaphysical-James Overreach.** `[KILL MODE]` *Test: does the writeup ride pragmatist truth into the claim that whether COVID was a leak is constituted by utility?* Remediation: stay on the axiology and the verification isomorphism. The microbiologist judge believes there is a mind-independent fact; relativism on that case loses them.

**Unmapped Vocabulary.** `[KILL MODE]` *Test: are trellis insider terms used without translation for judges who know epistemics but not the trellis?* Remediation: translate at first use. This is the largest standing risk given that the generality is obvious to the author and not to a stranger reading ten pages.

**False Isomorphism.** `[KILL MODE]` *Test: is the trellis-as-epistemic-stack mapping, or any structural correspondence, asserted without a worked demonstration?* Remediation: demonstrate the leg or strike it. Isomorphisms are earned, not forced.

**Naked Axioms.** `[FLAG MODE]` *Test: is a constraint presented in insider vocabulary where the audience needs the plain version?* Remediation: keep the constraint, add the translation. The flag-tier companion to Unmapped Vocabulary.

**Trellis-as-Hero.** `[FLAG MODE]` *Test: if every instance of the word "trellis" were deleted, would the submission still function as a method for navigating the case? If no, it is a product pitch.* Remediation: describe the moves, demote the brand. The correct posture is "here is what I did to navigate black holes, and here is how the moves generalize," not "here is the system, let me apply it." Testable only against prose, so it fires at draft review.

**Depth-as-Elaboration.** `[FLAG MODE]` *Test: does a deeper disclosure level add words rather than inferential substructure?* Remediation: decomposition adds structure (a claim opens into its supporting claims, evidence, and provenance); elaboration adds adjectives. The latter is Rhetorical Inflation wearing a depth costume. Every descent in the progressive-disclosure tool must expose more of the claim graph, never more rhetoric.

**False Symmetry.** `[FLAG MODE]` *Test: on a case whose terminal nodes are irreducible priors rather than measurements (COVID), does the graph hang both positions from equally weighted axioms and imply the question is a wash?* Remediation: the graph must carry the asymmetry, how much each weighting has to discount to survive, not just that a weighting exists. A branch on such a case is not closed, it is exposed, and honest exposure prices each side. Drawing a genuine evidential imbalance as fake even-handedness is the COVID-specific form of performed settling, the same sin as smearing a threshold into a confidence band one level up. Fires at draft review of any irreducible-prior leaf.

**Capability-Drift.** `[FLAG MODE]` *Test: does the submission claim the structure's value is an analytic insight (finding the correlated-evidence cascade, naming the load-bearing prior) that a capable single run already produces unaided?* Remediation: relocate the value to what a single run cannot be, the gate composing uncoordinated contributions. The off-the-shelf baseline moves; pin the distinctive claim to the compose, not the insight. Generated from the product post-mortem (CP-009, and the COVID leaf's CR-004), where the gate-uniqueness claim was falsified against a strong baseline.

**Ontology-Closure-Assumption.** `[FLAG MODE]` *Test: does an element assert the terminal ontology is complete (three types, or the empirical four), or claim sufficiency on a set a worked case already overflowed?* Remediation: state the ontology as open and extending on contact, with the worked extension instances named. The eggs sufficiency run and the COVID withheld-record gap are the two instances; claiming a closed set contradicts them. Generated from the product post-mortem (CP-008).

**Linter evolution via post-mortem.** Linter rules are retrospectively generated from post-mortems of significant failures. After resolving a major crisis, ask: what structural blindness allowed this error to persist? Can we encode a check that would have caught it at the gate? Each class-block kill in the Compost Pile is a candidate for a new Linter rule.

**Terminal-node typology, now an open ontology (Shore finding, revised 2026-06-26).** Running the method across the three children first showed they are not three instances of one demonstration but a typology of terminal nodes: the same engine, three exit conditions. Executing the leaves in the product carried the finding one step further and changed its shape. The ontology is not fixed at three, or at the empirical four. It extends on contact with a case it cannot type, and the extension is produced by the case reaching for a kind the current set lacks, not by an assertion. This was demonstrated twice. The eggs-harm question forced two new terminals (constitutive, derivation) when the empirical types left the two normative nodes reaching for an unavailable kind; the sufficiency was shown by running, with no ethical framework adopted. The COVID compose forced a third (withheld-record) when both emitters improvised a type for the same sealed-resolver situation and the gate flagged that its ontology had no canonical kind for it. One extension is canonized (constitutive, derivation), one is live and logged (withheld-record). That is the stronger generality claim: not "one engine, three exit conditions," but "one engine whose ontology is open and extends on contact, auditably, with two worked instances of the extension happening."
