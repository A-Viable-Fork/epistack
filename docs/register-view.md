# The Register View

## What this specifies, and the problem it closes

This defines a reading surface that presents the argument in one of two registers, an accessible register and the precise register the documents already carry, over the same underlying claims. It specifies the two registers and the cut between them, the fidelity discipline that makes the accessible register honest, the three link types that connect a reading to its source of truth, and the check that keeps the two registers from drifting apart. It defines the contract and the discipline; the prose of the accessible register is authored against this spec, not in it.

Three complaints converge on one surface. The submission argues teaching uplift and does not show it, so the claim reads as asserted while the reader's experience, density, contradicts it. The philosophical framing is dense enough that a reader meets the hardest possible reading at the front door. And the judges document is largely pointers to other documents, so a reader following a move cannot descend to where its truth lives at the point the move is made. These are one problem: the submission argues a market of views over shared trust, then foregrounds its single densest view as the entry. The register view is the submission demonstrating its own thesis on its own argument. A second register over the same trust is a second teacher over the same claims, which shows teaching instead of asserting it, makes density opt-in instead of mandatory, and is itself an instance of a view composing over shared trust without re-establishing it. The market of teachers stops being hypothetical the moment there are two.

## The register view is a view over shared trust

The register view reads through the propose and read contract (`api/client-api.mjs`, [`docs/api.md`](api.md)) and holds no claims and no grounding of its own, exactly as the presentation shell already does. It renders a chosen register of the same content the shell composes, so it is a presentation mode over the existing modules, not new content and not a second store. This placement is what makes building it a demonstration rather than chrome: a register is a view, the trust is untouched beneath it, and swapping registers is swapping presentation over one grounding, which is the trust-view cut ([`docs/trust-and-view.md`](trust-and-view.md)) operating on the argument's own prose.

The mechanism is not new. The vocabulary registers (`kernel/schema/registers.js`) already swap register at the grain of a single term: each concept carries a plain label, a literature label, and a delta, the thing its coinage adds so a register swap never flattens meaning. The register view lifts that same swap from term to passage. Term-grain register swap with a preserved delta becomes passage-grain register swap with a preserved delta, the same discipline at a coarser grain, which is why the fidelity mechanism below is the term-level delta grown up rather than a new invention.

## The two registers, and the cut between them

The precise register is the existing documents, unchanged. It is the source of truth for every claim and carries the full argument in its meditation register, built rather than asserted, with its resonances and its register-swaps intact. The accessible register is a new reading of the same claims in plain sentences, authored to be understood on first contact.

The cut between them is not claims against philosophy, and it is not essential concept against rhetorical density, because that binary breaks on the passages that matter most. It is fidelity of function against fidelity of form. The accessible register preserves everything an element does and states it in the plainest form that preserves the doing, and it declares the form it lost. The one question per element is whether it does work or only decorates. Decoration drops and is linked. Work is preserved, and work comes in four kinds, each with its own plainest-form handling, because the false move in the simpler binary is assuming that everything load-bearing reduces to a literal claim, and much of the load here does not.

An element that asserts is a claim, and its plainest form is its tightest statement. This is the easy case the simple binary handles: the separation of trust from view, the grounding rule that a claim is as grounded as its weakest necessary support, survive plain statement because they are the claims, and they pass into the accessible register stated plainly.

An element that constitutes is a figure with no literal floor beneath it, and its plainest form is a plainer figure with the over-commitment named. The definition of knowledge as what stands when trust in the knower is subtracted does not reduce to a literal claim: state it plainly and you get something like the part of a claim that stays justified when you stop believing whoever said it, which silently picks justified over warranted over holding, distinctions the figure is deliberately prior to. There is nothing underneath the figure to keep when it is stripped, so it is translated to a plainer figure rather than dropped or flattened, and the delta names exactly what the plainer figure over-commits to. The clearing is another such figure. These constitutive passages are where the delta note carries the most weight and, not coincidentally, where the structural check is blindest, so they are the highest-risk site for the accessible register drifting into the synthesis this submission critiques.

An element that supports by analogy is a claim wearing philosophy, and running the cut on it doubles as an audit. A Spinoza or Deleuze resonance is decoration if it dresses a claim and load-bearing if the argument leans on the mapping holding. Load-bearing, it is a claim, and a claim that load-bearing should be a grounded node rather than prose, so the cut forces the prior question the method already asks: is the isomorphism earned or forced. Forced, it does no work and drops as decoration. Earned, it is a claim that had better be grounded somewhere, and if it is not, the cut has surfaced a homeless truth or an ungrounded load-bearing move. So the works-versus-decorates pass is simultaneously an audit of whether the philosophy is carrying weight, which is the density the submission takes flak for.

An element that enacts a commitment is carried by cadence and must be promoted to a stated commitment, neither kept as rhythm nor dropped. "Concede the strength in full; the clearing is credible only if the strength is real" enacts sincerity through its rhythm; the claim is identical without the cadence, so the simple binary calls the cadence rhetorical and drops it, and the accessible reader gets a version that reads glib about synthesis and reopens the clever-dodge objection. The cadence was doing commitment work that is neither claim nor decoration, so the accessible register states the commitment the tone enacted, that the concession is sincere and the argument depends on synthesis genuinely being strong, in a plain sentence rather than a performed one.

One more kind is not an element but a shape: this prose builds by ramp, stating a claim and restating it at rising precision. Each restatement is individually removable, so the simple binary calls them all rhetorical, but the meditation register's habit is that the tightest statement lands last, as the payoff. So when a claim is ramped, the most precise statement is the claim and survives, the earlier ramps drop, and the delta records that the precise register builds the intuition by restatement. The last sentence, not the first.

This cut is itself a trust-view move. The philosophy is a view over the claims, not the knowledge, so making it opt-in is the submission applying its own thesis to its own prose, and the accessibility is bought by separating the presentation from the grounding exactly as the architecture separates them everywhere else.

## Fidelity has two tiers

Fidelity here is fidelity of function with declared loss of form. The accessible register keeps what every element does and states it in the plainest form that keeps the doing; the form it drops, a figure made plainer, a ramp collapsed to its payoff, a cadence turned into a stated commitment, is what the two tiers below make reachable and honest. The register is honest only if a reader can reach the form it set aside and is told, in the accessible text itself, what was set aside. Link-back handles the first. The named delta handles the second. Both are required, and the second is what keeps the accessible register from being a synthesis by another name, the single-user artifact this submission exists to critique.

**Availability, by link-back.** Two links run under a passage and they differ in kind, and keeping them distinct is what makes this the thesis rather than a convenience. The grounding link, to the passage's live node with its grade and its robustness, is not a property of either register. It is tied to the claim, so it runs under the precise register and the accessible one alike, and each register shows it at the depth that register needs, the precise reading beside its full-precision prose, the accessible reading beside its plainer prose, both standing on the same node. This is the invariant-and-view split the architecture draws everywhere: the grounding is the trust, invariant across presentations, and a register is a view over it, so the grounding is under both by construction, not carried into one as a feature. The register link is the one that is register-local. It runs from the accessible passage to the precise reading of the same passage, and it exists because the accessible register set form aside and the precise register set nothing aside, so only the accessible side needs a link back. It points at a version of its precise source, so if the precise register changes and the accessible one does not, the staleness is a checkable fact, the same versioned-pointer staleness a retail fork carries across a crossing. Precision is one click below comprehension, and grounding is one click below both.

**Honesty, by the named delta.** Where the accessible reading simplifies, it names what it drops, briefly, inline, so the plain statement cannot be mistaken for the whole claim. This is the term-level delta of `registers.js` lifted to the passage: the delta note states what the precise register carries that the accessible one set aside. The reader who never clicks still learns a true-though-incomplete claim that announces its own incompleteness, rather than a simplification passed off as the whole. Link-back makes precision reachable; the delta note makes the accessible text honest on its own.

## The cut is also a diet for the precise register

The authoring pass produces the accessible register, and it does a second thing on the way. The toggle hides density behind an opt-in, but hiding is the right treatment only for density that does work, a constitutive figure, an earned resonance, a ramp that builds intuition. Density that does no work is not something to hide, it is something to cut, and to cut from the precise register too, because decoration behind a toggle is still decoration. The works-versus-decorates pass is the scalpel that finds it, so running the cut audits the precise register at the same time it authors the accessible one: every element it marks decoration is a candidate for deletion in both, and every element it marks load-bearing but ungrounded is a finding, a forced isomorphism or a homeless truth, which is the philosophical density that reads as flak-bait precisely because it carries no weight.

This sets the accessible register's default form. It is not a translation of the precise prose into a foreign idiom, it is the submission restated in its own house style, lead with the answer, terse, affirmative, said once, the style the meditation register deliberately suspends to build a claim rather than assert it. The precise register earns that suspension where the density does work. Where it does not, the house style wins in the precise register too, and the cut is what tells the author where the suspension was earned and where it was habit. The practical read of the current prose is that pure decoration is rare and the two heavy categories are the ramp and the constitutive figure, so the largest accessibility lever is the ramp rule and leading with the claim, not figure-translation, which is the rarer and harder case.

## The three link types, kept distinct

Three kinds of link run through this surface and conflating them is why density and hyperlinks tangle. They are separated here.

The argument-chain link runs from a prose move to the source document it derives from. This link is free, because it is the `Depends on` edge already declared in the document headers and already checked for symmetry ([`docs/document-style-guide.md`](document-style-guide.md)). Carving the judges document's pointers further is surfacing these edges inline, at the point each derived move is made, so a reader following the argument descends to the source exactly where they meet the move, rather than finding a pile of pointers in a header.

The claim-to-node link runs from a prose claim to its live node, carrying that node's grounding and robustness. It is invariant across registers, because grounding is a property of the claim and not of how the claim is presented, so both the precise and the accessible reading of a case claim stand on the same node. What differs is depth of display, not whether the grounding is there: a register shows as much of the chain as its reader needs and no more, the precise reading able to walk the full descent, the accessible reading able to show the floor it reaches and leave the walk one click down, but neither reading is the grounded one and the other ungrounded. The shell already computes this cross-link for case content; the register view surfaces it under both registers rather than describing it in one.

The register link runs between the accessible and precise readings of one passage. This is the toggle, and it is the one new edge the register view adds.

## The drift guard

The two registers rot the way the CVD line rotted unless a check holds them together, so fidelity is asserted as a checked property, not a hope. A structural oracle, shaped like the document-graph check, verifies four things: every precise section has an accessible counterpart, every accessible section links its source, every accessible section carries its delta note, and every case-attached claim carries its grounding link under both registers, so the grounding is present wherever a claim names a node, in either reading, not in one. It also verifies the versioned pointer, flagging an accessible passage whose precise source has changed since the pointer was set. A submission that passes this check cannot silently drop a section, strip a source link, omit a delta, or leave one register grounded and the other bare, and cannot let the accessible register go stale against an edited precise one without the staleness showing.

The check's honesty is in what it does not verify, and this is the central bound of the whole design. A structural oracle can confirm that a delta note exists; it cannot confirm that the delta is complete, that it names everything the accessible passage actually dropped. It can confirm a counterpart exists; it cannot confirm the accessible reading entails no claim the precise one omits and omits no claim the precise one makes. Semantic fidelity, the accessible reading being true and its delta being honest, stays an authored property, verified against the precise source the way the case instances are verified against their literatures, and graded, not computed. The check is necessary and not sufficient: it prevents silent structural drift, and it makes the semantic obligation visible and located rather than discharging it. Claiming more would be the performed-settling the submission refuses.

## How it slots into the build

The register view is a view over the contract, thin and deterministic where it can be, or it is the interface polish the brief tells judges to discount. Built as a genuine view, reading through the propose and read contract, holding no grounding, rendering a chosen register of the shell's existing modules, it is spec rather than polish: a worked demonstration of the market thesis, the first second view over the trust. It is a toggle between two registers, not a continuous slider, for the same reason the kernel is top-down over three cases rather than an unbounded schema: exhibit the mechanism at minimum cost and let the market extend it. A reader defaults into the accessible register and opts into the precise one, and the default is what answers the density complaint: the density that does work is preserved entire, one toggle away, and the density that does none is cut by the same pass rather than hidden, so the precise register a reader opts into is the leaner one the diet leaves behind.

The built-versus-specified line holds here as everywhere. What is built when this ships is the register view as a mode over the judges document and the submission surface, with its link-back and its drift check running. What is not built is a plain twin of every document, which is unnecessary because the existing documents are the precise register by definition, so the accessible register is authored once, of the judges document and the surface, and links down into the precise sources that already exist.

## Scope against the deadline

The tractable floor is one accessible register of the judges document and the submission surface, its two fidelity tiers, and the drift check. That is authoring one register, not twinning the tree, and wiring one new link type plus one check.

The north star, named and not attempted before the deadline, is the judges argument itself structured as a typed claim graph, with the accessible and precise registers as two views over it, which is the submission finally applying its architecture to its own argument rather than only to its three cases. A partial spine of that graph, on the order of fifteen or twenty typed argument-claims for the load-bearing moves, may be tractable and would be the strongest version, since it would let the register view render from a grounded argument the way the case views render from grounded cases. It is the reach, not the floor, and it is marked as the reach so a slip on it costs nothing built.

## Open questions

Graded, with what would close each.

**The synthesis-by-another-name residual.** *Plausible that the discipline holds; the semantic half is the open seam.* The accessible register is exactly the artifact the submission critiques, a fluent single-author reading, unless the delta and the link and the check hold it to the claims. The structural check guarantees the scaffolding, that a counterpart, a link, and a delta exist. It does not guarantee the accessible reading is true or the delta complete, which stays authored and graded. Closes as far as it can close with a per-passage authored verification against the precise source, stated as a graded verdict per section, the way a case instance is verified; it does not close mechanically, and claiming it does would reintroduce the performed-settling being avoided.

**The works-versus-decorates adjudication.** *Confirmed as a usable test, marginal on two edges.* The one question, does the element do work or only decorate, decides most passages cleanly, and the four-kind typing of work, assert, constitute, support by analogy, enact, tells the author the plainest form to preserve. Two edges stay a judgment. The constitutive figure has no literal floor, so its plainer figure is chosen by the author and the delta records the over-commitment; a poor plainer figure is a fidelity loss the structural check cannot see, which is the residual below. The load-bearing resonance sits on the earned-versus-forced line, and a resonance mis-marked earned survives as a plainly stated claim that carries less than it seems to; the pass errs toward preserving and stating it, so the failure mode is a slightly denser accessible passage, not a lost claim, and the same pass flags it for grounding or for cutting. Closes per passage by author judgment; the test types the work and does not mechanize the cut.

**Semantic drift under edits.** *Partially closed by the versioned pointer.* The structural check catches a missing counterpart and a stale pointer, so an edit to a precise passage flags its accessible counterpart as stale. It does not catch an accessible passage rewritten to still-exist and still-point while no longer matching, which is the same authored-fidelity gap as the first question seen under editing. The versioned pointer bounds it to a flagged, locatable obligation rather than a silent one.

**Toggle versus gradient.** *Settled as a design decision, revisitable.* Two registers, not a slider, on the minimalism the whole submission follows. A gradient of registers is a larger market of views over the same trust and is exactly what the architecture enables later; it is deferred, not precluded, and the register view is built so a third register enters as another view rather than a rewrite.

**The typed-argument spine.** *Specified as the reach.* Whether the judges argument can be rendered as a grounded claim graph tractably before the deadline is open and not on the critical path. Closes with a partial spine authored and verified; until then the register view renders authored registers of the prose, which is the floor and is sufficient for the surface.

## Why this is the thesis applied to itself

Every part of this design is the architecture turned on its own argument. The accessible register is a view; the precise register is another view; the claims beneath both are the trust, established once and presented many ways. The grounding under both registers is the literal statement of the thesis, not an analogy to it: separate trust from view means the trust is the same object under every view, so a case claim is grounded in the accessible reading and the precise reading by the same node, and the registers differ only in how much of the grounding each shows its reader. A surface where the plain reading and the dense reading rest on one grounding is the trust-view cut made visible, and it is the answer to the reader who suspects the accessibility was bought by thinning the rigor: the rigor is the same node under both, one click down from either. The philosophy is separated from the claims as presentation is separated from grounding everywhere else. The delta is the register coinage's delta at a coarser grain. The link-back is the dependency graph surfaced at point of use. The drift check is the document-graph check extended to a second register. And the whole surface is the market of views made real on one instance, two teachers over one trust base, which is the demonstration the teaching criterion has been asking for and the argument has so far only made in words.
