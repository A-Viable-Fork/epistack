# Status Ledger

The single source of build truth. Every claim about what is and is not built lives here, graded on one scale, so prose elsewhere states a claim and points here instead of re-hedging in every paragraph. If a maturity claim appears anywhere else, it is wrong by construction: this file is the only place build status is asserted.

## How to read this ledger

The infrastructure is mapped in full, every function and component the three architecture documents name, organized into five build stages. A stage is a coherent slice of capability that stands on its own and that later stages build on. Stage 0 is the working kernel that ships today. Stages 1 through 4 lay out the path to the full system, each component named with its grade, so the scope of what remains is legible at a glance rather than discovered piecemeal.

Each component carries one grade:

**Built and verified.** Coded or stored, and the oracle (build, linter, test, or measurement) passes.
**In progress.** The boundary or scaffold exists and the remainder is named.
**Specified.** Designed and named, ready to build.
**A characterized leap.** A claim that reaches past its evidence, with the gap stated and the instance that would close it.

The unit of progress is the named component and the named gap. Within a stage, the open code obligations and deferred verifications live one level down in `docs/sorry-ledger.md`, machine-checked one-to-one with the markers in `data/`; this ledger tracks scope and maturity, the sorry ledger tracks the live obligations of whichever stage is in flight.

**Discipline.** A component is done when its line here moves, on the same merge that builds it.

The blueprint vocabulary is the spine. Three documents define the architecture: the functions (What), the makeup (How), and the rationale (Why). Component names below match them.

## Stage 0: The working kernel

The deterministic core operating over formal knowledge, with its contract boundary and its reading surfaces. This is the engine that ships. It grounds claims to a floor, detects gaps mechanically, composes contributions through a gate, preserves refutations, and exposes all of it through an open read and a gated write that clients consume. Demonstrated on three cases of different shape.

**[0.1] The typed claim store.** Storage is the canonical typed graph: claims as nodes, support as typed edges, one schema imported everywhere (`data/schema.js`, design axiom T0-1). Built.

**[0.2] The formal floor.** The measurement and proof terminals: cited primitive leaves, and the body corpus (Earth, the Sun, a white dwarf, a neutron star) as measurement-terminal leaves with value, unit, and citation (`data/bodies/bodies.js`). A claim grounds because the world or a proof closes it. Built and verified; dense-body scalars are order-of-magnitude placeholders flagged for tightening.

**[0.3] The grounding engine.** The detector that decides groundedness and finds the structural gaps: support reaching no terminal, a leaf that is not a cited primitive, a stale dependency, missing coverage, a dangling reference. Exposed as open API reads (`api.gaps`; `engine/gaps.js`). Run on the cases it reproduces the sorry ledger exactly, adds no false gaps, ranks nothing. Built and verified.

**[0.4] The composition gate.** The sole write path: two uncoordinated emitters' frozen output composes mechanically, no model in the loop (the v3 gate kernel, `kernel/gate/gate.mjs`, run headless by the oracles and live in the artifact's compose-gate panel, vendored offline to `vendor/gate/gate.bundle.js`; the Python compose-gate and its Pyodide runner are retired). The three cases are carried to the v3 model by a pure, deterministic trellis-to-v3 translator (`build/translate-trellis.mjs`, `docs/trellis-to-v3.md`) that reproduces the trellis grounding rather than re-establishing it: zero open gaps preserved, no divergence, LHC reaching measurement, COVID terminating at a priced-prior forum, eggs splitting (`build/check-translate.mjs`, `build/check-migrate.mjs`). Built and runnable.

**[0.5] The exclusion store.** Refuted claims kept with the reason and grounds, so a kill is recorded and cannot be silently revived (`docs/exclusion-reservoir.md`). Built.

**[0.6] The atlas.** Structural patterns made queryable, each carrying typed preconditions and their variants (`atlas.statistic-supports-conclusion` with its representativeness and sufficiency stages; `atlas.projectile-stops-in-target` with its density-regime variant). Load-bearing: the detector finds no coverage gap on either. Built and verified.

**[0.7] The perturbation overlay.** Flip an assumption and watch the conclusion give way: the engine COMPUTES the consequence cascade from the support graph (Prompt 21, `kernel/motions/perturb.js`, `api.perturb`) and returns a non-destructive overlay, displayed by the fat client. The computed rule is the same removal-and-recompute the robustness analysis runs for the worst single removal, aimed at a chosen flip; it propagates along support edges only and reproduces the authored cascade exactly, which is retained as the verification fixture. This discharges T1-3 in the exclusion reservoir: the rule audit exists and is asserted mechanically (`build/check-perturb.mjs`), so a wrong rule stops and reports rather than misleading silently. Pure, non-destructive, deterministic. Built and verified in-browser.

**[0.8] The store-and-API boundary.** Storage is the canonical graph; the engine exposes it through open reads and a gated write as one documented contract (`engine/api.js`, `docs/api.md`); consumers are clients of that API. Built. The interactive artifact is now a client over a provider-agnostic propose/read contract (`api/client-api.mjs`): the local provider runs the real v3 gate in-process over a frozen snapshot (`api/providers/local-provider.mjs`, `vendor/gate/snapshot.json`), the stub remote provider satisfies the same contract touching no kernel (`api/providers/remote-provider.mjs`), and the propose widget calls the contract and renders, computing no grounding, swapping providers by one import. The client-side receipt is byte-identical to a direct kernel run (`build/check-client.mjs`); the widget opens from `file://` with no console error. Built and verified in-browser.

**[0.9] The navigator.** The reading surfaces: the thin tier (a declarative manifest mapping node kind to look, validated loudly at build and render) and the fat tier (`client.teaching`, the learning walk; `client.auditor`, an inspect-and-dependents console), switchable in the shipped artifact, the boundary enforced by the linter so a client touches no truth field. The teaching client, the compare view (two cases breaking in the same structure at different stages), and the thin-client kit all ship. Built and verified in-browser. The submission itself is now a content-agnostic presentation shell (`periphery/navigate/shell/`) that composes registered modules, prose (the judges document rendered from its canonical source), case, and demonstration, into one navigable surface: the paper, the three cases, and the live demonstrations, cross-linked so a claim-reference in the prose carries its node's live grounding and robustness and the node links back. Every reading is read through the propose/read contract; the shell holds no content and no grounding logic (the stub-module test proves it stays untouched as content registers). The classic hand-authored submission is archived. Built and verified in-browser (`file://`, no console error).

**[0.10] The partition.** The auditable side (claims crossing to a measurement) and the priced-and-refused side (the off-path terminals) structurally separated, which grounds the no-verdict discipline. Built.

**Stage 0 open obligations** (in `docs/sorry-ledger.md`): none remain. All deferred verifications are discharged and the corpus is grounded to the floor on all three cases. The LHC case is authored across all three branches (production, stopping, accretion, the last read from Giddings-Mangano arXiv:0806.3381 rather than reconstructed from the Bondi reference form), with both Branch-2 checks verified against the same source: `lhc.N2.1` (the kinematic factor `gamma_BH ~ sqrt(s)/(2 m_N)`) at G-M eqs (5.3)-(5.5) and fig. 7, and `lhc.N2.2` (the charged/neutral split, charged: Earth and the Sun; neutral: white dwarfs and neutron stars) at G-M Sections 2.2 and 5.2. The two population-case instances are verified against their literatures: `covid.instance` against the epidemiology (Worobey et al. 2022; the ascertainment-bias critique, Weissman JRSS-A 2024, is well-founded and a leading critique among several co-equal ones, verdict marginal) and `eggs.instance` against the nutrition science (the dietary-cholesterol-to-LDL effect is real but modest, ~100 mg/day raises total cholesterol ~0.056 mmol/L, with about a third of individuals hyper-responders, so the population mean does not transfer to an individual, verdict confirmed). The generalization of the derivation segment to every measurement claim is a characterized leap, and the two-stage factoring of the contested statistic is a second; both close with further instances.

## Stage 1: The full grounding model

The lattice and the three grounding modes made explicit in the schema, turning the formal-first kernel into the three-mode kernel. This is what lets the system hold contested and declared knowledge with the same rigor it holds measured knowledge.

This stage is now realized by the v3 intake data model, which is canonical (`kernel/schema/confidence.mjs`, `kernel/grounding/earned-grade.mjs`, `kernel/gate/gate.mjs`; proven by `check-gate`, `check-migrate`, `check-robustness`). The Stage 1 trellis-era grounding that once sat beside it, the lattice, the mode registry, the per-mode check, and the contamination fold over the case graph, was **retired in Prompt 16** once the migration made v3 canonical, leaving one grounding implementation. The entries below read against v3.

**[1.1] The grounding lattice.** The ordering every claim sits in, from ungraded at the bottom, through the open line, to the settled tier, measuring how much a downstream claim may rely on this one. Built and verified as the v3 confidence order (`kernel/schema/confidence.mjs`): the enumerated lattice with meet, join, the collapse-to-one-settled, and the ceiling (the highest grade a kind can reach) fixed per kind; every claim's earned grade is derived against it (`kernel/grounding/earned-grade.mjs`) and read alongside it (the propose/read contract, `api.robustness`). The Stage 1 trellis lattice (`kernel/schema/lattice.js`) is retired.

**[1.2] The three grounding modes.** Formal (evidence or proof), constitutive (declaration), and forum (argument) as first-class modes. Built in the v3 order (`kernel/schema/confidence.mjs`): the settled tier branches into an empirical axis (checked < independently-rechecked) and a constitutive position incomparable to it, and the open line carries the forum grades; the earned-grade rule and the gate check groundedness per mode, refusing a cross-mode comparison as a decline rather than a failed test. The Stage 1 mode registry and per-mode stub (`kernel/schema/terminals.js`, `kernel/schema/modes.js`, `kernel/grounding/check.js`) are retired.

**[1.3] The forum region.** The contested region with its tiers, raw, attributed, structured, and its two subtypes: an open question whose ceiling is a floor (a real debt) and an unsettleable judgment whose ceiling is structured forum (settled once structured). In progress: the v3 order carries the open-line grades and a `forum` kind whose ceiling is corroborated, and the forum path is now exercised by a REAL case, the migrated COVID conclusion terminates at a priced-prior forum (`build/check-migrate.mjs`), not just a fixture. The first subtype, the debt whose ceiling is a floor, is now built as the **characterized gap** (Prompt 18, `kernel/analysis/characterized-gaps.mjs`): an empirical open-line claim with a floor ceiling, held by a transfer (an ordinary capping `supports` edge), carrying a closing condition that names the one measurement, proof, or direct study that would ground it (the sorry as a first-class field, mirroring the reinstatement condition, `kernel/schema/records.mjs`). The reading lists them as their own category and distinguishes a characterized gap from a bare assertion and a settled forum claim; it is exposed through the read contract (`api.characterizedGaps`) and proven on fixtures (`build/check-characterized-gaps.mjs`). The finer tier progression (raw, attributed, structured) as an authored ladder on real content remains.

**[1.4] The contamination rule.** The mechanical guarantee that weakness flows downward: a claim may never advertise more grounding than its supports provide. Built and verified in the v3 earned-grade rule (`kernel/grounding/earned-grade.mjs`): support delivery is the weakest-of within a group and strongest-of across groups, settledness is not inherited (a claim resting on settled support delivers corroborated, never the settled tier), and the earned grade is capped by the kind's ceiling; the gate admits a proposal only when its declared grade is at or below its earned grade (the `GM-ABOVE` rule, `kernel/gate/gate.mjs`), proven by `check-gate` and reproduced across the migrated cases by `check-migrate`. The independence pricing over disjoint source footprints, and the structural single-point-of-failure reading over the same support graph, are built in `check-robustness`. The Stage 1 contamination fold (`kernel/grounding/contamination.js`) is retired.

**[1.5] The edge taxonomy.** Edges as claims, typed and routed so an inference carries its own grounding. In progress: the v3 link kinds and their routing are built (`kernel/schema/records.mjs`, `kernel/gate/gate.mjs`): `supports` enters the grade computation, `depends-on` is a separate validity/presupposition check that does not deliver, and `contradicts`, `refines`, `restatement` route to contradiction, refinement, and deduplication; the robustness reading keeps the support and presupposition closures separate on this routing (`kernel/analysis/robustness.mjs`). `undercut` is now built as a reading (Prompt 25): it enters no grade fold in the gate, and `analyzeUndercuts` lowers the confidence an attacked claim transmits, one step per in-force undercut, over an added `undercut` link kind; the densified LHC case exercises it (Plaga on Leg 3, three undercuts on Leg 2). The remaining families (rebut-to-reconciliation, specialization, the discourse edges) remain specified.

**[1.6] The reconciliation register.** Conflict between two grounded claims held as a first-class disagreement with its crux extracted, rather than suppressed in favor of one side. Built (Prompt 22, `kernel/analysis/reconciliation.mjs`, kernel importing only the kernel; `build/check-reconcile.mjs`). A contradicts-linked pair is held as a disagreement record carrying both sides, their grades (derived on read, never stored), and the crux. The crux comes in two kinds and the reading routes by shape. A within-domain contradiction walks each side's support cone and returns the divergence frontier, where the cones stop sharing structure, as the crux candidate set, with the resolved sub-region separately; a cross-domain weighing names the framing node it rests on. The crux is a candidate, not a verdict, and a shallow frontier is a finding, not a failure. Exposed through the read contract (`api.reconciliations`) and surfaced in the eggs presentation. On the real CVD contradiction the computation first reported a shallow finding: the population null and harm claims rested on bare assertions, so the confounder had to be reified as a node before the crux computed meaningfully. Prompt 26 did that content act, reifying the confounding-adjustment as an explicit node both cohorts rest on (the US single-FFQ stance versus the global repeated-measures stance) plus the shared diabetic agreement and the mechanistic-floor parallel-rise, so the CVD crux now resolves: the divergence frontier is the confounding-adjustment choice and the diabetic phenotype sits in the resolved region (`build/check-eggs.mjs`, `build/check-reconcile.mjs`). The covid origins contradiction is the rich case (Prompt 23a, `corpora/covid/`, `build/check-covid.mjs`): the densified Rootclaim-debate ingest wires the zoonosis and lab-leak conclusions to rest on the same shared evidence and each on its own explicit priors, so the crux resolves to the priors, the divergence frontier is exactly the four prior claims and the shared evidence sits in the resolved sub-region. The write-up made the priors explicit precisely so this resolves; the case is surfaced through the presentation.

## Stage 2: Assessment, robustness, and ingestion

The structural assessments the kernel computes mechanically, the interpretive assessments the periphery contributes, and the pipeline that turns messy sources into claims. This is the layer that makes the engine useful on a raw, multi-source dispute.

**[2.1] The robustness analysis.** The reliability reading alongside grounding: the support graph read as a fault tree, a single point of failure found by re-derivation, robustness computed as the earned grade after the worst single removal, the fragility the interval between them. Run separately over support dependence and presupposition dependence for two distinct failure modes; the second finds a single point of well-formedness failure. Flags correlated evidence presented as independent, naming the shared claim. Built and verified (`kernel/analysis/robustness.mjs`, `build/check-robustness.mjs`): the four fixtures, the two-closure separation, and the three cases read concretely, the LHC antecedent a fragile conjunction with the N2.2 stopping mechanism the one genuinely redundant node, the COVID termination fragile at the exchangeability primitive. Exposed as an API read alongside grounding (`api/client-api.mjs` robustness). Derived on read, never stored; reports structural, not certified true, independence.

**[2.2] Mechanical assessment.** The argument-quality measures decidable from structure: double-counted evidence, missing coverage, and the set of claims affected if one falls. In progress: double-counted evidence is built as the correlated-evidence flag and the blast radius as the support closure, both in the robustness pass ([2.1], `kernel/analysis/robustness.mjs`), and missing coverage is the gap detector ([0.3], `kernel/analysis/gaps.js`); a single assessment surface that composes them into open reads remains. The Stage 2 stub `kernel/analysis/assessment.js` was retired in Prompt 16, its content now covered by robustness and the gap detector.

**[2.3] The ingestion pipeline.** The producer that reads messy multi-source material and emits attributed candidate claims with proposed types and links, then submits them through the gate. Extraction and first-pass typing live here; the gate makes them safe. This is the competition's ingestion layer. Specified.

**[2.4] The assessment agent.** The consumer that reads through the API and produces the interpretive assessments the kernel does not compute mechanically, the decisive question in a dispute, where a move carries more rhetorical than evidential weight, what to examine next, its conclusions submitted back through the gate as claims. Specified.

**[2.5] The query surface.** The discerning consumer's interface: whether a claim is grounded and where it is weakest, answered by walking the graph. Specified; thin layer over Stage 0 reads.

**[2.6] The red-team surface.** The tools for attacking the graph, proposing objections, finding thin grounding, submitting refutations, which operationalizes survival under scrutiny. Specified.

**[2.7] The forum filter.** The reader-side tier selection: the kernel keeps everything down to the raw tier, and the filter decides what a given reader sees, so openness at the bottom and strictness at the floor coexist. Specified.

## Stage 3: Composition and federation

Many sovereign kernels composing through a thin shared layer, so whole bodies of knowledge accrete by addition rather than by merger into one owned graph.

**[3.1] The composition protocol.** Cross-kernel citation as an edge whose target lives in another store, inheriting the target's grounding under that kernel's floors, plus the thin connective tissue: a shared way to express units and definitions so they translate, and boundary-dispute resolution. Built on fixtures (Prompt 19, `kernel/composition/`, kernel importing only the kernel; `build/check-composition.mjs`). A cross-store citation copies the domain store's grade and never assigns it (`records.mjs`, `transfer.mjs`); the grounding transfer is min(ceiling, min over necessary carried grades) under the v3 meet, so the settled tier collapses to one floor rank and the cross-boundary min is well defined. The shared vocabulary (`vocabulary.mjs`) declares a term once and references it by identity, selects the ceiling (a single shared term reaches the floor band, distinct terms cap at structured-forum), and detects the three divergences (version skew, cache drift, schema violation). The framing record and presupposition edge (`framing.mjs`) are the denominator seam: checked for in-force, never graded, so a fallen or swapped frame flags frame-orphaned and leaves the measurement's floor grade intact. Now run on real content (Prompt 20): the eggs case is restructured into a nutrition, an environment, and an economics domain store, ingested from the four research documents, and an eggs composite that cites them (`corpora/eggs/`, `build/check-eggs.mjs`, `build/eggs-build.mjs`). The domains ground to their own floors and the two cross-domain weighings (whether to eat eggs, which system is better) sit at structured-forum; the regenerative soil-carbon claims are characterized gaps, each a cross-system transfer capped below the floor with a closing condition; the denominator (product-throughput vs net-capital) is swappable and leaves the measurements intact. Prompt 26 deepens the nutrition domain into the three coupled subsystems an egg acts on (choline/one-carbon, metabolic regulation, the lipid mechanistic floor), so the case now carries four structural moves: the denominator reframe, the choline good-versus-bad fork (one nutrient held as a split, its TMAO causal link at the association grade with the fish-paradox, renal, and Mendelian undercuts), the cardiovascular crux (now resolving, [1.6]), and a second framing node, the which-body node (average adult / diabetic / hyper-responder / choline-deficient pregnant woman) whose swap reframes which effects dominate while the measurements hold. Built and verified on the real case, surfaced through the presentation shell (`periphery/navigate/shell/modules/eggs-case.js`, the vendored reading `vendor/eggs/reading.json`).

**[3.2] Subscription.** Notification when a relied-on claim changes across a boundary, a cited claim withdrawn, a gap closed, a grounding lowered, so a citing kernel never stands on a claim that has been pulled. Built on fixtures (Prompt 19, `kernel/composition/notify.mjs`, `profiles.mjs`; `build/check-composition.mjs`). A change notification drives re-derivation: affected citations are re-graded, re-pointed to a successor on the citing author's acceptance, or marked dangling; composite grades recompute by the record-2 rule and propagate transitively through internal support; a frame-status-change re-runs the presupposition checks. The re-derivation emits an auditable report that is itself a record (each affected claim's old and new grade, the dangling citations, the suspended claims, the newly frame-orphaned). Two grounding profiles read a domain store by its floor distribution and a composite store by its cited grounding, forum residents, citation health, and framing condition. Now run on the real eggs composite (Prompt 20): the composite grounding profile reports how much of the eggs case is inherited domain ground and how much is its own cross-domain forum. Computing the crux of a disagreement is now built (Prompt 22, [1.6]): the eggs case shows the cardiovascular contradiction with its computed crux beside it. With Prompt 26 the confounder is reified and the crux resolves to the confounding-adjustment choice, with the diabetic phenotype in the resolved region; the CVD structure is no longer a shallow finding.

**[3.3] Multiple kernels and grounding profiles.** The system running many kernels, each with its grounding profile (the distribution of formal, constitutive, and forum knowledge and where each sits), exposed as a read. A mathematics kernel is almost all formal; a nutrition-policy kernel is mostly forum with formal islands. Specified.

## Stage 4: The coordination fabric

The machinery that lets distributed parties write without anyone owning the result, and that makes capture of the commons recoverable. This is the unownable-graph design.

**[4.1] Layered access.** A private personal layer, an open forum layer carrying no verification weight, and the gated canonical layer. Specified.

**[4.2] Time-locked standing.** The non-transferable, decaying, domain-typed, revocable labor credential that gates the canonical write, earned by sampled verifiable work and priced in elapsed time so compute cannot front-load it. Specified.

**[4.3] The Knowledge Game.** The gated-write lifecycle: sponsorship under stake, fermentation in a cross-domain mesh that flags sterile agreement, and stratified random verification with asymmetric quorum. Specified.

**[4.4] The red-team immune system.** Super-stake challenges adjudicated by the standing population on rotation, bountied and anti-self-dealing. Specified.

**[4.5] The patch ledger.** Storage as a tamper-evident, append-only sequence of sealed canonical patches, so a captured present cannot un-verify the past. Specified.

**[4.6] Client-side patch selection.** Canonical as a per-client choice over the patch history, so a fork is a re-point rather than a rupture and no single gate can be owned. Specified.

### Open seams in Stage 4

Named risks in the coordination design, each with what would resolve it.

**[S1] The time-lock survival inequality.** Whether synthetic labor survives a long sampling-and-revocation window more cheaply than the value of capture. Parameterized; closes with the inequality stated and computed.

**[S2] Synthetic versus genuine harmonic.** Whether manufactured cross-domain disagreement is distinguishable from genuine. The cost argument is plausible; closes with the distinguishability result.

**[S3] Patch-boundary integrity.** Whether the patch boundary, as the rollback unit, is safe against poisoning the window before a seal. Rests on the append-only provenance envelope; closes by establishing that property rigorously.

**[S4] The density-collapse lever.** Whether an attacker can force collapse of a targeted sub-graph by inflating participant density and interaction velocity. Surfaced in adversarial review; open.

## The deeper assessment methods

Two research-grade methods sit above the staged build, in the subjective assessment layer, available to compute the harder readings once their open commitments are settled. Inconsistency: a justified confidence flow plus the discrete Hodge decomposition separates a consistent credence potential from local circular inconsistency and from globally irreducible tension; the harmonic part is the prize, pending a justified metric on the edges. Topological holes: adjudications as 2-cells, with the first Betti number counting loops of relation no adjudication spans; pending a commitment to what fills a loop. Both are research, and both would compute the cross-cutting quantities, how load-bearing a claim is, how faithful an isomorphism is, that the staged build leaves to judgment.
