# Status Ledger

## In one read

This ledger is the transparency criterion operationalized, not a list of gaps. It applies to the submission the same discipline the kernel applies to a claim: state plainly what is built and what is not, and let the boundary between them be a checkable fact rather than a rhetorical choice.

Read the built-versus-specified split as a deliberate choice, not as unfinished business. The built core is Stage 0, the working kernel and its checks (the schema, the gate, the grounding rule, the readings, the three cases, the presentation surface, all coded with oracles that pass), together with the now-built composition and federation of Stage 3: the content-addressed crossing, the agreement gate, the kernel generator, and the bottom-up federation of the four cases as standalone members, each with a passing oracle. That built core is the contribution. The specified frontier is the Stage 4 coordination fabric, the machinery that governs writes to a federation at scale (the time-locked credential, the gated-write lifecycle, the challenge system), deferred on purpose with [S1] chief among the open seams. Building it before the deadline would trade a clean, honestly bounded core for a rushed one, which is exactly the performed-settling the whole submission argues against, so it is named as a deferral with its open questions stated.

So a specified stage here is a named-on-purpose deferral, not a claim in flight. This summary states the split and its reason and stops: it does not claim or imply that any specified stage is nearly done or as good as built, and it does not soften what is unbuilt. Read what is built and load-bearing first; then read the specified stages as the mapped path they are, not as a shortfall.

The single source of build truth. Every claim about what is and is not built lives here, graded on one scale, so prose elsewhere states a claim and points here instead of re-hedging in every paragraph. If a maturity claim appears anywhere else, it is wrong by construction: this file is the only place build status is asserted.

The repository holds its own governing constraints as a trellis, `docs/governing-trellis.md`, enforced by the same check suite, each constraint's enforcement status derived from the oracle that checks it.

## How to read this ledger

The infrastructure is mapped in full, every function and component the three architecture documents name, organized into five build stages. A stage is a coherent slice of capability that stands on its own and that later stages build on. Stage 0 is the working kernel that ships today. Stages 1 through 4 lay out the path to the full system, each component named with its grade, so the scope of what remains is legible at a glance rather than discovered piecemeal.

Each component carries one grade:

**Built and verified.** Coded or stored, and the oracle (build, linter, test, or measurement) passes.
**In progress.** The boundary or scaffold exists and the remainder is named.
**Specified.** Designed and named, ready to build.
**A characterized leap.** A claim that reaches past its evidence, with the gap stated and the instance that would close it.

The unit of progress is the named component and the named gap. Within a stage, the open code obligations and deferred verifications live one level down in `docs/sorry-ledger.md`, machine-checked one-to-one with the markers in `data/`; this ledger tracks scope and maturity, the sorry ledger tracks the live obligations of whichever stage is in flight.

**Discipline.** A component is done when its line here moves, on the same merge that builds it.

The blueprint vocabulary is the spine. Three documents define the architecture: the functions (What), the makeup (How), and the rationale (Why). Component names below match them.

## Stage 0: The working kernel

The deterministic core operating over formal knowledge, with its contract boundary and its reading surfaces. This is the engine that ships. It grounds claims to a floor, detects gaps mechanically, composes contributions through a gate, preserves refutations, and exposes all of it through an open read and a gated write that clients consume. Demonstrated on three cases of different shape.

**[0.1] The typed claim store.** Storage is the canonical typed graph: claims as nodes, support as typed edges, one schema imported everywhere (`data/schema.js`, design axiom T0-1). Built.

**[0.2] The formal floor.** The measurement and proof terminals: cited primitive leaves, and the body corpus (Earth, the Sun, a white dwarf, a neutron star) as measurement-terminal leaves with value, unit, and citation (`data/bodies/bodies.js`). **[0.3] The grounding engine.** The detector that decides groundedness and finds the structural gaps: support reaching no terminal, a leaf that is not a cited primitive, a stale dependency, missing coverage, a dangling reference. **[0.4] The composition gate.** The sole write path: two uncoordinated emitters' frozen output composes mechanically, no model in the loop (the v3 gate kernel, `kernel/gate/gate.mjs`, run headless by the oracles and live in the artifact's compose-gate panel, vendored offline to `vendor/gate/gate.bundle.js`; the Python compose-gate and its Pyodide runner are retired). Built and runnable.

**[0.5] The exclusion store.** Refuted claims kept with the reason and grounds, so a kill is recorded and cannot be silently revived (`docs/exclusion-reservoir.md`). Built.

**[0.6] The atlas.** Structural patterns made queryable, each carrying typed preconditions and their variants (`atlas.statistic-supports-conclusion` with its representativeness and sufficiency stages; `atlas.projectile-stops-in-target` with its density-regime variant). ## Stage 1: The full grounding model

The lattice and the three grounding modes made explicit in the schema, turning the formal-first kernel into the three-mode kernel. This is what lets the system hold contested and declared knowledge with the same rigor it holds measured knowledge.

This stage is now realized by the v3 intake data model, which is canonical (`kernel/schema/confidence.mjs`, `kernel/grounding/earned-grade.mjs`, `kernel/gate/gate.mjs`; proven by `check-gate`, `check-migrate`, `check-robustness`). The Stage 1 trellis-era grounding that once sat beside it, the lattice, the mode registry, the per-mode check, and the contamination fold over the case graph, was **retired in Prompt 16** once the migration made v3 canonical, leaving one grounding implementation. The entries below read against v3.

**[1.1] The grounding lattice.** The ordering every claim sits in, from ungraded at the bottom, through the open line, to the settled tier, measuring how much a downstream claim may rely on this one. Built and verified as the v3 confidence order (`kernel/schema/confidence.mjs`): the enumerated lattice with meet, join, the collapse-to-one-settled, and the ceiling (the highest grade a kind can reach) fixed per kind; every claim's earned grade is derived against it (`kernel/grounding/earned-grade.mjs`) and read alongside it (the propose/read contract, `api.robustness`). The Stage 1 trellis lattice (`kernel/schema/lattice.js`) is retired.

**[1.2] The three grounding modes.** Formal (evidence or proof), constitutive (declaration), and forum (argument) as first-class modes. Built in the v3 order (`kernel/schema/confidence.mjs`): the settled tier branches into an empirical axis (checked < independently-rechecked) and a constitutive position incomparable to it, and the open line carries the forum grades; the earned-grade rule and the gate check groundedness per mode, refusing a cross-mode comparison as a decline rather than a failed test. The Stage 1 mode registry and per-mode stub (`kernel/schema/terminals.js`, `kernel/schema/modes.js`, `kernel/grounding/check.js`) are retired.

**[1.3] The forum region.** The contested region with its tiers, raw, attributed, structured, and its two subtypes: an open question whose ceiling is a floor (a real debt) and an unsettleable judgment whose ceiling is structured forum (settled once structured). In progress: the v3 order carries the open-line grades and a `forum` kind whose ceiling is corroborated, and the forum path is now exercised by a REAL case, the migrated COVID conclusion terminates at a priced-prior forum (`build/check-migrate.mjs`), not just a fixture. The first subtype, the debt whose ceiling is a floor, is now built as the **characterized gap** (Prompt 18, `kernel/analysis/characterized-gaps.mjs`): an empirical open-line claim with a floor ceiling, held by a transfer (an ordinary capping `supports` edge), carrying a closing condition that names the one measurement, proof, or direct study that would ground it (the sorry as a first-class field, mirroring the reinstatement condition, `kernel/schema/records.mjs`). The reading lists them as their own category and distinguishes a characterized gap from a bare assertion and a settled forum claim; it is exposed through the read contract (`api.characterizedGaps`) and proven on fixtures (`build/check-characterized-gaps.mjs`). The finer tier progression (raw, attributed, structured) as an authored ladder on real content remains.

**[1.4] The contamination rule.** The mechanical guarantee that weakness flows downward: a claim may never advertise more grounding than its supports provide. Built and verified in the v3 earned-grade rule (`kernel/grounding/earned-grade.mjs`): support delivery is the weakest-of within a group and strongest-of across groups, settledness is not inherited (a claim resting on settled support delivers corroborated, never the settled tier), and the earned grade is capped by the kind's ceiling; the gate admits a proposal only when its declared grade is at or below its earned grade (the `GM-ABOVE` rule, `kernel/gate/gate.mjs`), proven by `check-gate` and reproduced across the migrated cases by `check-migrate`. The independence pricing over disjoint source footprints, and the structural single-point-of-failure reading over the same support graph, are built in `check-robustness`. The Stage 1 contamination fold (`kernel/grounding/contamination.js`) is retired.

**[1.5] The edge taxonomy.** Edges as claims, typed and routed so an inference carries its own grounding. In progress: the v3 link kinds and their routing are built (`kernel/schema/records.mjs`, `kernel/gate/gate.mjs`): `supports` enters the grade computation, `depends-on` is a separate validity/presupposition check that does not deliver, and `contradicts`, `refines`, `restatement` route to contradiction, refinement, and deduplication; the robustness reading keeps the support and presupposition closures separate on this routing (`kernel/analysis/robustness.mjs`). `undercut` is now built as a reading (Prompt 25): it enters no grade fold in the gate, and `analyzeUndercuts` lowers the confidence an attacked claim transmits, one step per in-force undercut, over an added `undercut` link kind; the densified LHC case exercises it (Plaga on Leg 3, three undercuts on Leg 2). The remaining families (rebut-to-reconciliation, specialization, the discourse edges) remain specified.

**[1.6] The reconciliation register.** Conflict between two grounded claims held as a first-class disagreement with its crux extracted, rather than suppressed in favor of one side. Built (Prompt 22, `kernel/analysis/reconciliation.mjs`, kernel importing only the kernel; `build/check-reconcile.mjs`). A contradicts-linked pair is held as a disagreement record carrying both sides, their grades (derived on read, never stored), and the crux. The crux comes in two kinds and the reading routes by shape. A within-domain contradiction walks each side's support cone and returns the divergence frontier, where the cones stop sharing structure, as the crux candidate set, with the resolved sub-region separately; a cross-domain weighing names the framing node it rests on. The crux is a candidate, not a verdict, and a shallow frontier is a finding, not a failure. Exposed through the read contract (`api.reconciliations`) and surfaced in the eggs presentation. On the real CVD contradiction the computation first reported a shallow finding: the population null and harm claims rested on bare assertions, so the confounder had to be reified as a node before the crux computed meaningfully. Prompt 26 did that content act, reifying the confounding-adjustment as an explicit node both cohorts rest on (the US single-FFQ stance versus the global repeated-measures stance) plus the shared diabetic agreement and the mechanistic-floor parallel-rise, so the CVD crux now resolves: the divergence frontier is the confounding-adjustment choice and the diabetic phenotype sits in the resolved region (`build/check-eggs.mjs`, `build/check-reconcile.mjs`). The covid origins contradiction is the rich case (Prompt 23a, `corpora/covid/`, `build/check-covid.mjs`): the densified Rootclaim-debate ingest wires the zoonosis and lab-leak conclusions to rest on the same shared evidence and each on its own explicit priors, so the crux resolves to the priors, the divergence frontier is exactly the four prior claims and the shared evidence sits in the resolved sub-region. The write-up made the priors explicit precisely so this resolves; the case is surfaced through the presentation. Prompt 34 deepens the covid case from a deep extraction (`corpora/covid/covid-depth.js`) under the existing spine, not replacing it: each of the six lines runs several nodes deep (the ZO reading, the LL counter, the counters to those); three careful readers (Alexander, Van Treuren, Stansifer) weight the same evidence as attributed forum nodes carrying the likelihood ratios (20,000x, 10,000x, 500x, 20x, 1x), stated-versus-reconstructed intact; the reader divergences on shared evidence (Eric versus Will on the furin site and on the two lineages) are first-class contradictions, the prior structure appearing between the assessors and not only the debaters; and each line's sub-crux is wired, three bottoming out in a withheld-record terminal (the sealed December-2019 case data, the WIV sequencing database, the WIV internal databases), typed as such and surfacing as characterized gaps, while the temporal and environmental sub-cruxes bottom out in empirical records that ground and the two-lineage sub-crux in contested bioinformatics. The crux now resolves at more than one depth: the top origin crux still to the four priors, a line-level crux to that line's sub-crux, landing on different terminal types. The deepening stays even-handed on the structure (both origins are argued on every line) while reporting the real ZO lean in the sources rather than balancing it with invented nodes (`build/check-covid.mjs`).

## Stage 2: Assessment and robustness

The structural assessments the kernel computes mechanically, and the interpretive assessments the periphery contributes. This is the layer that makes the engine useful on a raw, multi-source dispute.

**Outside the protocol: the periphery workflows.** Producing typed claims from messy sources is the claimant's work, performed in the periphery and converging at the one built gate; the producer workflows (video, web, structured data) are documented in the workflow atlas (`docs/workflow-atlas.md`), anchored by the built connectors (the ingestion connector and the producer MCP tool, [0.9], with their passing checks). The consumer workflows (the evidence finder, the crux locator, the adversarial auditor, the reader-side forum filter, and the AI reader) are assessment-layer clients over the built read contract, documented in the atlas, the architecture documents, and the AI-consumer documents; the store deliberately computes structure and defers interpretation and presentation to them. The protocol's contribution is the gate and the store, the two layers neither side of the boundary can supply for itself.

**[2.1] The robustness analysis.** The reliability reading alongside grounding: the support graph read as a fault tree, a single point of failure found by re-derivation, robustness computed as the earned grade after the worst single removal, the fragility the interval between them. Run separately over support dependence and presupposition dependence for two distinct failure modes; the second finds a single point of well-formedness failure. Flags correlated evidence presented as independent, naming the shared claim. Built and verified (`kernel/analysis/robustness.mjs`, `build/check-robustness.mjs`): the four fixtures, the two-closure separation, and the three cases read concretely, the LHC antecedent a fragile conjunction with the N2.2 stopping mechanism the one genuinely redundant node, the COVID termination fragile at the exchangeability primitive. Exposed as an API read alongside grounding (`api/client-api.mjs` robustness). Derived on read, never stored; reports structural, not certified true, independence.

**[2.2] Mechanical assessment.** The argument-quality measures decidable from structure: double-counted evidence, missing coverage, and the set of claims affected if one falls. In progress: double-counted evidence is built as the correlated-evidence flag and the blast radius as the support closure, both in the robustness pass ([2.1], `kernel/analysis/robustness.mjs`), and missing coverage is the gap detector ([0.3], `kernel/analysis/gaps.js`); a single assessment surface that composes them into open reads remains. The Stage 2 stub `kernel/analysis/assessment.js` was retired in Prompt 16, its content now covered by robustness and the gap detector.

## Stage 3: Composition and federation

Many standalone kernels composing through a thin shared layer, so whole bodies of knowledge accrete by addition rather than by merger into one owned graph.

**[3.1] The composition protocol.** Cross-kernel citation as an edge whose target lives in another store, inheriting the target's grounding under that kernel's floors, plus the thin connective tissue: a shared way to express units and definitions so they translate, and boundary-dispute resolution. Built on fixtures (Prompt 19, `kernel/composition/`, kernel importing only the kernel; `build/check-composition.mjs`). A cross-store citation copies the domain store's grade and never assigns it (`records.mjs`, `transfer.mjs`); the grounding transfer is min(ceiling, min over necessary carried grades) under the v3 meet, so the settled tier collapses to one floor rank and the cross-boundary min is well defined. The shared vocabulary (`vocabulary.mjs`) declares a term once and references it by identity, selects the ceiling (a single shared term reaches the floor band, distinct terms cap at structured-forum), and detects the three divergences (version skew, cache drift, schema violation). The framing record and presupposition edge (`framing.mjs`) are the denominator seam: checked for in-force, never graded, so a fallen or swapped frame flags frame-orphaned and leaves the measurement's floor grade intact. Now run on real content (Prompt 20): the eggs case is restructured into a nutrition, an environment, and an economics domain store, ingested from the four research documents, and an eggs composite that cites them (`corpora/eggs/`, `build/check-eggs.mjs`, `build/eggs-build.mjs`). The domains ground to their own floors and the two cross-domain weighings (whether to eat eggs, which system is better) sit at structured-forum; the regenerative soil-carbon claims are characterized gaps, each a cross-system transfer capped below the floor with a closing condition; the denominator (product-throughput vs net-capital) is swappable and leaves the measurements intact. Prompt 26 deepens the nutrition domain into the three coupled subsystems an egg acts on (choline/one-carbon, metabolic regulation, the lipid mechanistic floor), so the case now carries four structural moves: the denominator reframe, the choline good-versus-bad fork (one nutrient held as a split, its TMAO causal link at the association grade with the fish-paradox, renal, and Mendelian undercuts), the cardiovascular crux (now resolving, [1.6]), and a second framing node, the which-body node (average adult / diabetic / hyper-responder / choline-deficient pregnant woman) whose swap reframes which effects dominate while the measurements hold. Built and verified on the real case, surfaced through the presentation shell (`periphery/navigate/shell/modules/eggs-case.js`, the vendored reading `vendor/eggs/reading.json`). Prompt 35 names the composition contract as a governance layer in `docs/parameters-register.md`: the line the protocol draws between a free tier a community configures to its own situation (time locks, standing rules, agent policy, type system) and a thin required tier every kernel must hold to stay composable (typed claims, monotone grounding, the untyped type grounding nothing, claims carrying history, forkable standing). Agent policy sits in the free tier, which is the worked consequence of producer-agnosticism: the core checks a claim's structure rather than the claimant's nature, so any differential treatment of agents is a configured parameter the standing system makes enforceable, not an architectural given. Documentation of an existing design property, not new kernel behavior.

**[3.2] Subscription.** Notification when a relied-on claim changes across a boundary, a cited claim withdrawn, a gap closed, a grounding lowered, so a citing kernel never stands on a claim that has been pulled. Built on fixtures (Prompt 19, `kernel/composition/notify.mjs`, `profiles.mjs`; `build/check-composition.mjs`). A change notification drives re-derivation: affected citations are re-graded, re-pointed to a successor on the citing author's acceptance, or marked dangling; composite grades recompute by the record-2 rule and propagate transitively through internal support; a frame-status-change re-runs the presupposition checks. The re-derivation emits an auditable report that is itself a record (each affected claim's old and new grade, the dangling citations, the suspended claims, the newly frame-orphaned). Two grounding profiles read a domain store by its floor distribution and a composite store by its cited grounding, forum residents, citation health, and framing condition. Now run on the real eggs composite (Prompt 20): the composite grounding profile reports how much of the eggs case is inherited domain ground and how much is its own cross-domain forum. Computing the crux of a disagreement is now built (Prompt 22, [1.6]): the eggs case shows the cardiovascular contradiction with its computed crux beside it. With Prompt 26 the confounder is reified and the crux resolves to the confounding-adjustment choice, with the diabetic phenotype in the resolved region; the CVD structure is no longer a shallow finding.

**[3.3] Multiple kernels, federated.** Built (the bottom-up meta kernel). The four cases run as standalone members of a federation joined at the untyped type, each owning its own kind table and pinning its own type-hashes, never a merged schema. `build/check-bottomup.mjs` proves their independence adversarially rather than asserting it: the four members own separate schemas (lhc holds `derivation` and lineage holds `declaration`, each held by no other member, so this is not four relabeled regions of one schema), standing crosses only through an owned fork (an unpinned crossing arrives untyped and grounds nothing, and the try-to-cheat step confirms no unforked path to standing before a fork restores it), and same-hash crossings compose native and lossless on real content. The composite selects among the four members per question and grounds by citing across the boundary, and the transparency status is default-on and reported. Built and verified (`build/bottomup-build.mjs`, `build/check-bottomup.mjs`, `corpora/federation/federation.js`). The remaining specified piece is the per-kernel grounding-profile read across a running federation (the distribution of formal, constitutive, and forum knowledge, exposed per member): a mathematics kernel is almost all formal, a nutrition-policy kernel mostly forum with formal islands.

**[3.4] The kernel generator and the shared type tier.** Built. Content-addressed type bundles (`kernel/schema/type-hash.mjs`) hash a type together with the apparatus that gives it meaning, so two kernels that mean the same thing by a type pin the same hash and one that means something different pins a different one; `build/check-type-hash.mjs` proves determinism and meaning-sensitivity, and `build/check-crossing.mjs` proves same-hash crossing composes native and lossless while an unpinned crossing arrives untyped and a fork restores standing. The shared type subtree is seeded by the common kinds (`corpora/_shared/common-types.js`), and all four cases are verified to agree on them four-way (`build/check-agreement.mjs`), which is what lets the subtree publish them as shared. The generator (`scaffolder/new-kernel.mjs`) instantiates a kernel from a config, emitting its tables, an empty claims-and-links skeleton, and a build-and-check pair, then runs the real gate and the real check over its output; it holds no copy of the rules, so a source class outside the substrate menu or a type the kernel never adopted fails the real check with the named cause. Proven by regenerating a case's schema from config, the eggs tables reproduced exactly (same adopted hashes, same kinds, same sources). The three tiers the config draws are named as data: the pinned substrate (the grade order, the gate, the crossing contract, the floor and source-class and link-kind menus), the shared subtree (content-addressed bundles adopted by pinning a hash), and the local free tier (the kernel's own kinds, sources, and content), with the transparency machinery inherited and default-on. Specified in this area: the full publish/adopt lifecycle for custom types (publishing floors and source classes as shared bundles under their own hashes) and the interactive layer over the generator. Built generator and crossing; specified publish/adopt lifecycle. (`kernel/schema/type-hash.mjs`, `corpora/_shared/common-types.js`, `scaffolder/kernel-config.schema.json`, `scaffolder/new-kernel.mjs`, `build/check-type-hash.mjs`, `build/check-crossing.mjs`, `build/check-agreement.mjs`.)

**[3.5] The vocabulary kernel, the fifth exhibit.** Built. The submission's own terms are a kernel: each term is a `declaration` claim, the kind whose ceiling is the constitutive floor, so a definition stands by a public act of adoption rather than by evidence. Two tiers ground two ways. A core term (the submission owns it) grounds by adoption in its home document, the region that defines it; a reference term (the submission does not own it) grounds in an external citation, the honest floor for a borrowed word (a physics reference for cross-section, a logic reference for modus tollens). The oracle `build/check-vocabulary.mjs` verifies exactly what adoption makes checkable and no more: every core term's cited home region actually contains the term, so a definition cannot drift from the document that grounds it (the drift guard, register-fidelity made mechanical), and every reference term carries a resolving source, never an invented one. It does not recompute earned grades, because adoption is not modeled as support edges here; the declared grade is the floor the kind sets, not a promotion. Overloaded words carry one claim per sense, each with its own home; the leakage terms from an earlier decomposition are barred from becoming claims. Built and verified (`corpora/vocabulary/vocabulary.js`, `corpora/vocabulary/tables.js`, `build/check-vocabulary.mjs`: 123 terms, 107 core in their home region, 16 reference in external citation). One known deferral: the AI-agent sense is authored as the term `LLM`, since the standalone phrase "language model" is not yet present in any home document to ground a core term of that name.

## Stage 4: The coordination fabric

The machinery that lets distributed parties write without anyone owning the result, and that makes capture of the commons recoverable. This is the unownable-graph design. The fabric is now developed as a worked specification in `docs/coordination-layer-spec.md`, which designs each mechanism to the level of the function it must perform and the fixed properties any filling must hold, with the forms left free, and shows the layer growing from the built grade computation extended from claims to participants. It remains specified, not built: the spec makes the frontier legible without closing it, and every entry below keeps the status it holds here.

**[4.1] Layered access.** A private personal layer, an open forum layer carrying no verification weight, and the gated canonical layer. Specified.

**[4.2] Time-locked standing.** The non-transferable, decaying, domain-typed, revocable labor credential that gates the canonical write, earned by sampled verifiable work and priced in elapsed time so compute cannot front-load it. Specified. `docs/coordination-layer-spec.md` develops the mechanism as standing-is-a-weighting-not-a-currency read from an active-tenure record, with the fixed property a concave threshold-free weighting that decays with inactivity; it leaves the curve's form and steepness free and names that steepness as a real openness-against-security tradeoff on its own frontier rather than resolving it.

**[4.3] The Knowledge Game.** The gated-write lifecycle: sponsorship under stake, fermentation in a cross-domain mesh that flags sterile agreement, and stratified random verification with asymmetric quorum. Specified.

**[4.4] The red-team immune system.** Super-stake challenges adjudicated by the standing population on rotation, bountied and anti-self-dealing. Specified. `docs/coordination-layer-spec.md` develops the mechanism as the opposed random draws on every claim, at least one random verifier and at least one random red-teamer, whose opposition is what removes collusion without trusting anyone to be fair; the fixed property is that there is at least one unsteerable draw of each opposed role, with how many beyond the minimum left to a community's contract.

**[4.5] The patch ledger.** Storage as a tamper-evident, append-only sequence of sealed canonical patches, so a captured present cannot un-verify the past. Specified.

**[4.6] Client-side patch selection.** Canonical as a per-client choice over the patch history, so a fork is a re-point rather than a rupture and no single gate can be owned. Specified.

### Open seams in Stage 4

Named risks in the coordination design, each with what would resolve it. `docs/coordination-layer-spec.md` gives these seams a designed context rather than a closure: it treats standing as a weighting read from a record and freezes each community's parameterization as a governance-hash so standing composes across communities, and it names the same open questions as its own frontier, the joint stability of several floating parameters as a per-configuration property to be tested not claimed, the best form for each slot as unknown and left to the market of governance-hashes, the steepness of the concave weighting as a genuine tradeoff, the seed corpus as an honest genesis, and tradable stake as the line past which standing becomes a currency, held beyond scope. The seams stay open; the spec makes them legible.

**[S1] The nonemptiness of the lever space.** The time-lock and standing are levers a community sets to price gaming above the value of capture while keeping the price of honest contribution bounded (the free tier, `docs/parameters-register.md`). The direction is an argument, not a pending computation: standing priced in elapsed time rises with the window while capture value is fixed, so a longer window raises the cost of gaming. What stays open is whether the window that beats capture for a given stake and adversary is also short enough that honest standing survives its own decay, so the usable interval is nonempty. This is conditional on the sampling the levers rely on being unfakeable, so [S1] rests on [S2] and [S4] rather than beside them. Closes with the interval shown nonempty for a stated stake and adversary, under a sampling-integrity result from [S2].

**[S2] Synthetic versus genuine harmonic.** Whether manufactured cross-domain disagreement is distinguishable from genuine. The cost argument is plausible; closes with the distinguishability result.

**[S3] Patch-boundary integrity.** Whether the patch boundary, as the rollback unit, is safe against poisoning the window before a seal. Rests on the append-only provenance envelope; closes by establishing that property rigorously.

**[S4] The density-collapse lever.** Whether an attacker can force collapse of a targeted sub-graph by inflating participant density and interaction velocity. Surfaced in adversarial review; open.

## The deeper assessment methods

Two research-grade methods sit above the staged build, in the subjective assessment layer, available to compute the harder readings once their open commitments are settled. Inconsistency: a justified confidence flow plus the discrete Hodge decomposition separates a consistent credence potential from local circular inconsistency and from globally irreducible tension; the harmonic part is the prize, pending a justified metric on the edges. Topological holes: adjudications as 2-cells, with the first Betti number counting loops of relation no adjudication spans; pending a commitment to what fills a loop. Both are research, and both would compute the cross-cutting quantities, how load-bearing a claim is, how faithful an isomorphism is, that the staged build leaves to judgment.
