# EpiStack

EpiStack is a protocol for structure, realized in three layers: a kernel that holds contested questions as typed, checkable objects, a contract that makes the kernel's boundary real, and a periphery kept deliberately small to show what the first two enable. The contribution lives in the kernel. To see why structure is the contribution worth making, begin by clearing synthesis.

Synthesis is what the strongest tools do, and they do it well. A good synthesis reads cleanly, integrates across sources, surfaces connections a reader would have missed, and the machines do all of this better each month. Concede the strength in full; the clearing is credible only if the strength is real. Every form of synthesis is organized for a reader who supplies the trust. Its structure lives in the prose, or in the head of the model that wrote it, so the reader receives sentences and a request for belief, and checking any one step means reconstructing the whole from the sources. A synthesis therefore stands exactly as long as its reader's trust does: it compounds only as accumulated prose, and under challenge it dissolves back into its bibliography. Cheap generation finishes the clearing. When a plausible synthesis costs nothing to produce, the trust it asks for is the entire product, and that trust now hangs on air.

What remains is the floor: knowledge is the part of a claim that stands when trust in whoever asserts it is subtracted. A measurement repeats. A proof verifies. A declaration is a public act whose issuance anyone can confirm. Claims that rest on argument stay in the open, in a forum, their weighings visible and marked unsettled. Some floors are high and some are low; an honest engine reports the height.

EpiStack builds at that floor, and its three layers are the protocol's anatomy.

The kernel is the contribution. It stores claims as a typed graph with the support between them. Each claim grounds to a floor, a measurement, a proof, or a declaration, or sits in the forum when argument is what it rests on. A new claim enters through a gate and is admitted when it holds together with what is already there. Standing is a property of the graph, propagated by one rule: a claim is as grounded as its weakest necessary support, and contested support carries its contest forward. Any reader can recompute any claim's standing from scratch while trusting no one. That recomputation is the checkable object the clearing asked for, and it is what lets structure compound: new claims build on standings that survive the same subtraction that admitted them.

The API is the contract that makes the boundary real. Everything above reads the kernel through it, and everything that enters the kernel enters through the gate. The boundary is where trust is subtracted, so the contract is where the protocol holds.

The periphery is minimal by choice. It is the smallest surface that demonstrates what a checkable kernel enables downstream: composition across domains, presentation, a walkable path through the cases. A thick periphery would be claiming to be a product; the honest demonstration of a protocol is the smallest surface that shows what the kernel allows. The thin periphery is an existence proof that tools become cheap once the structure is checkable, and it is swappable: anyone can build a richer surface on the same kernel through the same contract, and improving models and interfaces will.

Three cases run on the engine, and each yields something a good synthesis would have smoothed flat. The LHC safety argument looks robust, several independent lines converging, until the graph reifies the dependency the lines share as a node and the convergence is repriced as one assumption wearing several coats. The COVID origins evidence looks decisive, in whichever direction the teller favors, until the prior the verdict turns on is named as a claim of its own and the debate resolves into a disagreement over one weighing, held in the open where a reader can reprice it. The egg nutrition verdict looks like a fact until the frame it presupposes, which population, which endpoint, which food it replaces, is swapped and the verdict swaps with it. Three cases, three buried things surfaced: the critique of synthesis and the proof of the kernel are the same three motions.
