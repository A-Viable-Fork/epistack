# EpiStack

This submission is two of the shapes the brief names: a critique with counterexamples, and the protocol it forces.

There are two ways into this submission: read the critique below and click any claim to land on the grounding beneath it, its grade and where it can fail, computed from the graph rather than asserted in the text; or verify the protocol by running the oracles, each of which states the invariant it checked.

Synthesis is the most a single model can do, and the strongest tools do it well. A good synthesis reads cleanly, integrates across sources, surfaces connections a reader would have missed, and the machines do all of this better each month. That strength is real and standing. Its limit is structural: a synthesis is one viewpoint's reach, so from inside it you cannot separate what the world is from what is the vantage, because the synthesizer is woven through the view and cannot be subtracted from within. Everything downstream follows from that. The reader supplies the trust the prose asks for, checking any step means reconstructing the whole from the sources, and under challenge the synthesis offers the synthesizer's authority in place of a shared floor. Cheap generation sharpens this rather than softening it: the better and cheaper the synthesis, the clearer that even the best is still one viewpoint, and the trust it asks for is the whole product.

Knowledge is the invariant left as the model is attenuated. It is composed of syntheses and identical to none. It is what a synthesis builds from, the established ground each new view starts on, so synthesis and knowledge are a cycle: knowledge is composed of syntheses and is the ground the next synthesis starts from, and no single synthesis is itself knowledge. Trust in the knower is removed by replacing it with a warrant that does not need the knower. One exception secures itself: where a claim carries its own verification, a proof asks you to trust nothing about the prover, only to check the steps, so one proof plus its review subtracts the prover from inside a single synthesis, and formal domains are the case where the synthesizer self-eliminates. Everywhere else, empirical and contested, a synthesis cannot secure itself, so the warrant is the cross-viewpoint invariant: one physicist's measurement is testimony you either trust or do not, but many independent measurements compose into knowledge that holds when no single measurer is load-bearing. A synthesis can supply neither warrant on its own, because its synthesizer is only trusted, never verified and never subtracted. The structure supplies both: the gate verifies what secures itself, and composition subtracts the knower from the rest.

The kernel is the machine that performs the attenuation, and typing is how. To type a claim as a measurement is to subject it to the measurement attenuation, driving its dependence on its author down toward the measurement floor; the type sets the form the attenuation takes, completing to zero where the type self-secures, which is the proof's subtraction, and approaching a floor where it does not. The machinery is a typed graph, a gate that admits only contributions holding together with what is already there, and one propagation rule, a claim is as grounded as its weakest necessary support, contested support carrying its contest forward, so any reader recomputes any claim's standing from scratch while trusting no one. Watch it work and notice what it never knows: the kernel has no idea what a claim is about, nor what produced it. Floors are domain-specific, a measurement in physics, an estimate in epidemiology, a declaration for a definition; the kernel treats any floor uniformly. It is producer-agnostic too: it checks a claim's structure, not the claimant's nature, so a producer, anything that emits claims, human or model, is checked the same. Two axes have traveled under one word, trust and domain, and pulling them apart is the design. There is one kernel in the trust sense, and it is domain-agnostic by construction.

An agnostic core admits more than one arrangement of domains above it, and the arrangements sit on one axis, the direction of authority over the schema. A domain-specific kernel covers one subject with its own schema and floors, the base unit. A bottom-up meta kernel federates independent domain kernels that keep their own schemas, joined at a shared untyped type, authority staying with the members. A top-down meta kernel starts from one schema spanning the domains as regions within it, authority in the schema, composition native. The untyped type is the hinge that makes this an axis: one type every schema shares, holding foreign imports and locally untyped claims alike, grounding nothing because it is the type no attenuation has touched, so standing crosses only when an author forks it into a local type, retail per claim or wholesale per category, and retail forks accumulating into wholesale ones is how shared vocabulary grows from use. Each end has its regime: top-down buys coherence and native composition at the price of one schema, right for a single author spanning a unified analysis, which is why this submission builds it; bottom-up buys federation and incremental adoption at the price of lossy crossings, right for many authors who will never agree on types. The interface market, one grounded core under many surfaces you read and write through a single contract, holds at either end.

EpiStack is the top-down point on that axis, built for its regime, and the three cases are the proof it does real epistemic work. Each yields the counterexample a good synthesis would have smoothed flat. The LHC safety argument looks robust, several lines converging, until the graph reifies the dependency the lines share as a node and the convergence reprices as one assumption wearing several coats. The COVID origins evidence looks decisive, in whichever direction the teller favors, until the prior the verdict turns on is named as a claim of its own and the debate resolves into one weighing, held in the open where a reader can reprice it. The egg nutrition verdict looks like a fact until the frame it presupposes, which population, which endpoint, which food it replaces, is swapped and the verdict swaps with it. Three domains, three floors, three counterexamples, one schema that never knew the difference.

The critique is complete, and the climb it forces is built: the top-down instance, the generator that instantiates the type from a config, and the four cases federated bottom-up as standalone members, each step checked by an oracle, with the coordination fabric that governs writes at scale specified above it. The periphery around the kernel is thin by choice, the smallest surface that shows what a checkable core enables, reading through one contract so richer tools swap in as models improve. And the schema is a base others can fork: the untyped type already sits at the bottom of the ordering, the hinge a federation of many authors will need. The top-down kernel and the four-case federation are what was built; the federation of many authors is where it points.
