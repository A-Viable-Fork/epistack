# EpiStack

This submission is two of the shapes the brief names: a critique with counterexamples, and the protocol it forces. The critique is below, placing synthesis: knowledge is what stands when trust in the author is removed, and no synthesis can remove it from inside one viewpoint. The protocol is a climb the critique forces: the knowledge kernel type, one instance built and checked, a generator that instantiates the type from a config, and the four cases federated as standalone kernels to prove the generator makes real ones. The critique is complete; the climb is built through the generator and its federation, with the coordination fabric that governs writes at scale specified above it.

EpiStack is a knowledge kernel, a type of epistemic infrastructure: a fixed core that declares grounding and lints it in CI, with two independent markets over it, a market of kernels to trust and a market of surfaces to read and write through. This submission builds one arrangement of that type, a top-down meta kernel, one designed schema for claims and their grounding, demonstrably spanning three domains that share nothing, physics measurement, epidemiological contestation, normative weighing. The top-down arrangement is the setting a sole author fits to a competition asking for one checkable analysis, not the contribution; the contribution is the type. To see why, begin by placing synthesis.

There are two ways into this submission: read the argument and click any claim to land on the grounding beneath it, its grade and where it can fail, computed from the graph rather than asserted in the text; or verify the protocol by running the oracles, each of which states the invariant it checked.

Synthesis is the most a single reasoner can do, and the strongest tools do it well. A good synthesis reads cleanly, integrates across sources, surfaces connections a reader would have missed, and the machines do all of this better each month. That strength is real and standing. Its limit is structural: a synthesis is one viewpoint's reach, so from inside it you cannot separate what is the world from what is the vantage, because the synthesizer's trust is woven through the view and cannot be subtracted from within. Everything downstream follows from that. The reader supplies the trust the prose asks for, checking any step means reconstructing the whole from the sources, and under challenge the synthesis offers the synthesizer's authority in place of a shared floor. Cheap generation sharpens this rather than softening it: the better and cheaper the synthesis, the clearer that even the best is still one viewpoint, and the trust it asks for is the whole product.

Knowledge is what stands across viewpoints, the invariant left when each synthesizer's trust is subtracted, composed of syntheses and identical to none. It is also what a synthesis builds from, the established ground each new view starts on, so synthesis and knowledge are a cycle: knowledge is composed of syntheses and is the ground the next synthesis starts from, and no single synthesis is itself knowledge. Trust in the knower is removed by replacing it with a warrant that does not need the knower. One exception secures itself: where a claim carries its own verification, a proof asks you to trust nothing about the prover, only to check the steps, so one proof plus its review subtracts the prover from inside a single synthesis, and formal domains are the case where the synthesizer self-eliminates. Everywhere else, empirical and contested, a synthesis cannot secure itself, so the warrant is the cross-viewpoint invariant: one physicist's measurement is testimony you either trust or do not, but many independent measurements compose into knowledge that holds when no single measurer is load-bearing. A synthesis can supply neither warrant on its own, because its synthesizer is only trusted, never verified and never subtracted. The structure supplies both: the gate verifies what secures itself, and composition subtracts the knower from the rest.

The kernel is the machinery that grounds claims to floors: a typed graph, a gate that admits only contributions holding together with what is already there, and one propagation rule, a claim is as grounded as its weakest necessary support, contested support carries its contest forward, and any reader recomputes any claim's standing from scratch while trusting no one. Watch it work and notice what it never knows: the kernel has no idea what a claim is about, nor what produced it. Floors are domain-specific, a measurement in physics, an estimate in epidemiology, a declaration for a definition; the kernel treats any floor uniformly. It is also producer-agnostic: it checks a claim's structure, not the claimant's nature, so a producer, meaning anything that emits claims, human or model, is checked the same. Two axes have traveled under one word, trust and domain, and pulling them apart is the design. There is one kernel in the trust sense, and it is domain-agnostic by construction.

An agnostic core admits more than one arrangement of domains above it, and the arrangements sit on one axis, the direction of authority over the schema. A domain-specific kernel covers one subject with its own schema and floors, the base unit. A bottom-up meta kernel federates independent domain kernels that keep their own schemas, joined at a shared untyped type, authority staying with the members. A top-down meta kernel starts from one schema spanning the domains as regions within it, authority in the schema, composition native. The untyped type is the hinge that makes this an axis: one type every schema shares, holding foreign imports and locally untyped claims alike, grounding nothing, so standing crosses only when an author forks it across, retail per claim or wholesale per category, and retail forks accumulating into wholesale ones is how shared vocabulary grows from use. Each end of the axis has its regime: top-down buys coherence and native composition at the price of one schema, right for a single author spanning a unified analysis, which is why this submission builds it; bottom-up buys federation and incremental adoption at the price of lossy crossings, right for many authors who will never agree on types, which a different entrant should build from the same knowledge kernel type. The arrangement is a fitted choice on the trust axis; the interface market, one grounded core under many surfaces you read and write through a single contract, holds at either end.

EpiStack is the top-down point on that axis, built for its regime, and the three cases are the proof it does real epistemic work. Each yields the counterexample a good synthesis would have smoothed flat. The LHC safety argument looks robust, several lines converging, until the graph reifies the dependency the lines share as a node and the convergence reprices as one assumption wearing several coats. The COVID origins evidence looks decisive, in whichever direction the teller favors, until the prior the verdict turns on is named as a claim of its own and the debate resolves into one weighing, held in the open where a reader can reprice it. The egg nutrition verdict looks like a fact until the frame it presupposes, which population, which endpoint, which food it replaces, is swapped and the verdict swaps with it. Three domains, three floors, three counterexamples, one schema that never knew the difference.

The periphery around the kernel is thin by choice, the smallest surface that shows what a checkable core enables, reading through one contract so richer tools swap in as models improve. And the schema is a base others can fork: the untyped type already sits at the bottom of the ordering, which is the hinge a federation of many authors will need. The top-down kernel is what was built; the bottom-up federation is what it points to.
