# The self-kernel, stage 0: the frame

The self-kernel is the reflexive corpus: epistack's claims about itself as a running, self-describing
system, grounded through its own gate like any other corpus. It sits beside the case rooms (eggs, covid,
lhc, lineage) as content the engine judges, not as machinery. That placement is the point. The protocol
submits its self-description to its own gate, at no privileged position: a self-claim earns exactly the
grade its cited evidence earns, and where it asserts more than a theorem or a check grounds, it floors and
becomes a characterized gap, the honest map of what the protocol has not yet proved about itself.

## What it governs

The protocol's claims about its own properties, its own definitions, and its own theses, in three
registers, the trichotomy the decomposition established.

- **Invariant claims** (measurement kind): the protocol's structural properties as claims about a running
  system. Each cites its grounding: a math-kernel theorem by ref for why the property must hold, and a
  check for that the code holds it. A claim may cite both. The gate computes each grade; a grounded
  invariant reaches checked, and an invariant the suite does not yet ground floors with a ledger entry.
- **Constitutive claims** (declaration kind): the definitions the invariant and evaluative claims
  presuppose, adopted not evidenced, each citing the vocabulary kernel's existing entry rather than
  re-authoring it. They ground at the constitutive floor by adoption.
- **Evaluative claims** (forum kind): the theses, at the forum floor, contestable, their grounding absent
  by design because they await a community's semantic judgment, exactly as the submission overview's
  evaluative claims do.

A small tagged subset is `entrance-surfaced` for the org-root entrance, so the protocol's front door
renders from its own grounded claims rather than hand-written copy.

## What it does not govern

The case domains (eggs, covid, lhc, lineage), which are their own kernels with their own subjects; the
math kernel and the vocabulary kernel, which this kernel cites rather than restates; and the engine's
code, which the checks test but which is not itself a claim. The reflexive subject is the whole point:
this is the kernel whose domain is the system it runs in, and it holds no more standing over that system
than the gate grants any other claimant.

## Store

`store_id` is `S-self`. The data is `corpora/self/self-data.js`; the builder is `build/self-build.mjs`;
the oracle is `build/check-self.mjs`, which resolves every citation (each math-theorem ref exists in the
math kernel and grounds, each cited check exists, each constitutive ref exists in the vocabulary kernel),
lets the gate compute every grade, and fails on a dangling citation, an overclaim, or a definitional
constitutive claim with no vocabulary entry.
